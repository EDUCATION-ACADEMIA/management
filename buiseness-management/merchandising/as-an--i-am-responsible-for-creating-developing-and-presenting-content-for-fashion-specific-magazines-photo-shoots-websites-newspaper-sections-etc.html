As an ____ I am responsible for creating, developing and presenting content for fashion-specific magazines, photo shoots, websites, newspaper sections, etc.

(A) Stylist

(B) Editor

(C) ** Fashion Director

(D) Art Director

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A fashion editor is a person that supervises the process of creating, developing and presenting content for the fashion department of a magazine, Web site, newspaper or television program. The work of a fashion editor can be quite varied and may hold several responsibilities.

Concept note-2: -Fashion Editors are generally responsible for supervising the overall process of creating, planning, developing, managing and presenting content for fashion-specific magazines, photo shoots, websites, newspaper sections, or television shows.
