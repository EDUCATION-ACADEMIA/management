The visual merchandiser prepares the new spring merchandise plan for Foot Locker shoe and apparel retailer. Which tool should be used?

(A) stock keeping unit (SKU)

(B) small business association (SBA)

(C) ** computer aided design (CAD)

(D) universal product code (UPC)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A visual merchandiser needs a solid technical basis (color theory, lighting technology), visual culture and artistic flair, but also some knowledge of marketing and psychology. A good store display should engage as many senses as possible, and not only sight.

Concept note-2: -Visual merchandising is a marketing practice that uses floor plans, color, lighting, displays, technology, and other elements to attract customer attention. Its ultimate purpose is to use the retail space to generate more sales. A visual merchandiser is the person behind the magic.

Concept note-3: -Formal balance is achieved by placing large items with large items and small items with small items. Informal balance is achieved by balancing a large item with several smaller ones in a display.
