Macy’s is a company that sells products to end users (consumers). Which type of intermediary is Macy’s?

(A) Consumer

(B) Manufacturer

(C) ** Retailer

(D) Wholesaler

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Macy’s is an American-based department store dealing in retail-based and fashion-related products, such as footwear, clothing, and accessories. Sears is also a retail-based American company that deals in a wide range of products related to furniture and fashion, among others.

Concept note-2: -If the warehouse and the retail trade have a material group in different variations, this is referred to as a deep assortment. The best examples of this are a bakery or a fish shop. The many kinds of bread or fish make the mixture a deep offer.

Concept note-3: -One of retailers’ most fundamental activities is providing the right mix of merchandise and services that satisfies the needs of the target market. The only consumer advantages to shopping in a brick-and-mortar store are browsing and the ability to touch and feel the products.
