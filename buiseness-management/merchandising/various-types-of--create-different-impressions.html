[SOURCES]
Various types of ____ create different impressions.

(A) colors

(B) shapes

(C) forms

(D) ** lines

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Straight vertical lines produce an impression of uprightness and stability. They can also imply height and vertical movement. In contrast, straight horizontal lines create impressions of flatness. They can also imply lateral space and a passive state. Diagonal lines produce an impression of diagonal movement or motion.

Concept note-2: -Display & Video 360 has several types of line items for different media buys: Display for image, HTML5 (including rich media), and native ads (both display and video). Video for video ads.

Concept note-3: -A marketing impression is defined as the number of opportunities people have to view your campaign. So, using our billboard illustration, the impressions would be how many times people passed the sign-regardless of how often they drove by it or even glanced at the ad.
