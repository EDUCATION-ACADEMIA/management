Michael Jackson and Madonna were fashion icons in the ____

(A) 60’s

(B) 70’s

(C) ** 80’s

(D) 90’s

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Madonna. From wearing underwear as outerwear to her trademark lacy gloves, crucifix chains and love of Boy London jeans-Madonna was an undisputed fashion icon. Most 80s clothing worth wearing was worn by this absolute icon.

Concept note-2: -In the 1980s, Michael began a fashion revolution for himself, embracing a slimmer silhouette, shorter pants, bright-colored socks and, most importantly, sequins. This is also around the time when some of Michael’s most recognizable pieces of clothing found their way into pop-culture history.

Concept note-3: -The 1980s saw the emergence of electronic dance music and new wave, also known as Modern Rock. As disco fell out of fashion in the decade’s early years, genres such as post-disco, Italo disco, Euro disco, and dance-pop became more popular.

Concept note-4: -Just think about it: the ‘80s brought us fishnets and legwarmers, served up electric colorways and the naked dress, made a case for leggings-as-pants, and inserted shoulder pads into any blazer and blouse that designers would allow.
