Lydia was unsure what to buy her brother for his birthday. When shepassed Hollister, a clothing store, she noticed a leather jacket in thestore window with a 20% off sign. This is an example of which type of display?

(A) Institutional

(B) Island

(C) ** Promotional

(D) Showcase

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Directional and departmental signage are usually large and placed fairly high, so they can be seen throughout the store. Category signage is usually smaller and is intended to be seen from a shorter distance; they are located on or close to the fixture itself where the merchandise is displayed.

Concept note-2: -Personal selling is the most expensive of the five promotional elements per contact.

Concept note-3: -Display * refers to the visual and artistic aspects of presenting a product to a target group of customers. Visual merchandising, by contrast, encompasses the visual and artistic aspects of the entire business environment. The visual and artistic aspects of presenting a product to a target group of customers.

Concept note-4: -All four elements work together to create an overall image designed to appeal to the target customer. The storefront leads the customers into the store and other elements, such as layout and interior displays, encourage them to stay there and purchase items.
