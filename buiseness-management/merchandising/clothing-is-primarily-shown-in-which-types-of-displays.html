Clothing is primarily shown in which types of displays?

(A) Middle-of-aisle and end-of-aisle displays

(B) Open and end-of-aisle displays

(C) Architectural and closed displays

(D) ** Wall and open displays

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The four basic types of displays are those that feature one item; similar products; related products; and a cross mix of items.

Concept note-2: -The five types of digital product displays are CRT, LCD, LED, plasma, and OLED.

Concept note-3: -A retail display is a physical structure or space used to promote and sell merchandise in a retail environment. Retail displays can be found in both physical stores and digital stores. They are typically used to showcase products in an appealing way, to attract customers and encourage them to make a purchase.

Concept note-4: -Display Settings: background or type of setting for presenting merchandise or display.
