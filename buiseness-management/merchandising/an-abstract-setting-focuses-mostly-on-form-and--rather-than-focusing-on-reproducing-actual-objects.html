An abstract setting focuses mostly on form and ____ rather than focusing on reproducing actual objects.

(A) size

(B) width

(C) line

(D) ** color

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rather than focusing on the truthful depiction or realistic imitation of an object, abstract art looks at other non-objective artistic elements of shape, form, color, and line. Abstract techniques have been used by artists to explore ideas beyond the canvas and our physical reality.

Concept note-2: -The primary characteristic of abstract art is that It is characterized by the use of form, color, and line in a way that does not represent or depict specific objects or scenes.

Concept note-3: -The opposite of representational art is complete abstraction, where the lines, colors, and shapes themselves are the focus of the artwork, rather than any existing thing. This is known as non-representational art.
