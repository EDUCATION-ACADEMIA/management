what is window display in visual merchandising

(A) ** The first piece of visual merchandising customers encounter with a brand is the window display.

(B) window display with attraction

(C) window display whereby visual Merchandising is place on

(D) the brand and concept will reveal by visual merchandisers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first piece of visual merchandising customers encounter with a brand is the window display. Window displays are used as an initial attraction to bring customers into a store and are also used as a marketing tool to communicate the brand’s image to the consumers as well as to distinguish itself from its competitors.

Concept note-2: -Window displays are a form of advertisement that allow stores to display what they have to offer, both in stock and creativity. How a store chooses to design their window display shows potential customers how much effort they put into their business and the quality of service customers will receive.

Concept note-3: -noun. (Retail: Merchandising) A window-display in a store window shows examples of the goods sold in that store.

Concept note-4: -Closed Window Displays This type of display is an excellent choice if a brand wants to draw attention directly and exclusively to the products on display without distracting customers with other merchandising systems inside the store.

Concept note-5: -Window displays preview what’s inside your shop. You can use them to entice customers with products they’ll like and to highlight new arrivals, limited-edition products, or even holiday gifts.
