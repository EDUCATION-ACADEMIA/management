Styles that are popular for a brief period of time, usually for only one selling season, are which response to fashion?

(A) Classics

(B) Flops

(C) Long-run fashions

(D) ** Short-run fashions

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Terms in this set (13) The direction in which a particular change or fashion is moving. Classic. A style that stays in fashion for a long time. Fad. Something new in clothing that quickly becomes popular, but only for a short time.

Concept note-2: -A fashion trendâ€™s life cycle can be divided into five stages, generally speaking: introduction, rise, peak, decline, and obsolescence. The life cycles of fashion trends today have changed; technology and social media have rendered them much shorter and less predictable than in the past.

Concept note-3: -The process by which a particular design, activity, color, etc., comes into some popularity and then phases out.

Concept note-4: -Obsolescence: The fifth stage of the fashion cycle when the style is rejected, is undesirable at any price, is no longer worn, and is no longer produced.
