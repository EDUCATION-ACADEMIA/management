Macy’s department store wants displays that can stand alone on the sales floor in the entrance. This describes which type of window display?

(A) Enclosed

(B) ** Island

(C) Open

(D) Semi-enclosed

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An attractive, appealing display of merchandise combined with effective store layout and decor is: visual merchandising.

Concept note-2: -Marketing Essentials Chapter 18, Section 18.1. Page 9. Storefront. A marquee is an architectural canopy that extends. over a store’s entrance.

Concept note-3: -Formal balance is achieved by placing large items with large items and small items with small items. Informal balance is achieved by balancing a large item with several smaller ones in a display.

Concept note-4: -Personal selling is the most expensive of the five promotional elements per contact.
