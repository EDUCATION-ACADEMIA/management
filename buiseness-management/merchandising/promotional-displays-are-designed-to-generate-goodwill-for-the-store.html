Promotional displays are designed to generate goodwill for the store.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A news release refers to any activity designed to create goodwill toward a business.

Concept note-2: -Institutional Advertisements: The objective is to build goodwill or an image for an organization rather than promote a specific good or service.

Concept note-3: -Advertising is a way of marketing your business in order to increase sales or make your audience aware of your products or services. Until a customer deals with you directly and actually buys your products or services, your advertising may help to form their first impressions of your business.

Concept note-4: -Promotion is any communication used to inform, persuade, and/or remind people about an organization’s or individual’s goods, services, image, ideas, community involvement, or impact on society.
