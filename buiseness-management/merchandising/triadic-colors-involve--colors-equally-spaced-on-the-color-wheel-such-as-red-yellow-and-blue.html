Triadic colors involve ____ colors equally spaced on the color wheel, such as red, yellow and blue.

(A) four

(B) five

(C) ** three

(D) six

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A triadic color scheme uses three-spaced colors on the color wheel. Red, yellow, and blue are three primary colors forming a triadic color scheme. Analogous colors create a calming effect, while triadic colors are more vibrant.

Concept note-2: -Triadic: A triadic color scheme uses three colors that are evenly spaced apart on the color wheel. The green-purple-orange palette is a common example of a triad.

Concept note-3: -Here are perfect examples of triadic color combinations: Purple, Green, and Orange. Blue-Violet, Red-Orange, and Yellow-Green. Red-Violet, Yellow-Orange, and Blue-Green.

Concept note-4: -All triadic color examples consist of three colors that are evenly spaced out on the traditional color wheel. Black and/or white may also be present within a triadic harmony.

Concept note-5: -A triad is a color scheme, a special variant of the split-complementary color scheme, with the equal distance between all colors. All three colors are distributed evenly around the color wheel, causing there is no clear dominance of one color.
