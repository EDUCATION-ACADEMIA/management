Which type of promotion do businesses generally have the least control over?

(A) Direct mail

(B) Outdoor signage

(C) Personal selling

(D) ** Publicity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Businesses spend more money promoting to consumers than to other businesses. An advantage of publicity is that it costs less than other forms of promotion.

Concept note-2: -Advertising is a form of nonpersonal promotion in which companies pay to promote ideas, goods, or services. These promotions are presented through a variety of media outlets.

Concept note-3: -Personal selling is the most expensive of the five promotional elements per contact.

Concept note-4: -An attractive, appealing display of merchandise combined with effective store layout and decor is: visual merchandising.
