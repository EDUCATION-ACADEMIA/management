Which is the action of studying market conditions, consumer lifestyles, current events, and the mood of the public to provide reports for apparel markets?

(A) Customer profiling

(B) ** Forecasting

(C) Market segmentation

(D) Test marketing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fashion forecasting involves the following activities such as studying market conditions, noting the life style of the people, researching sales statistics, evaluating popular designer collections, surveying fashion publications, observing street fashions etc.

Concept note-2: -A fashion forecaster predicts the colors, patterns, fabrics, textures, materials, prints, graphics, beauty grooming, accessories, footwear, street styles and many other styles that will be presented on different runway shows and in stores in upcoming seasons.

Concept note-3: -Trend forecasting in fashion aims to create a story using shapes, design elements, colors, fabrics, and trims. Trend forecasters make reports and mood boards that designers use to create new clothing and accessories for brands.

Concept note-4: -Fashion merchandisers may work within retail operations as buyers who select product lines and create promotions to enhance both brand and market share. Others work directly for designers and fashion labels placing products in stores, finding shelf space, and managing marketing and promotions.

Concept note-5: -Trend Forecasting is the process of researching and formulating predictions on consumers future buying habits. By identifying the source, tracing the evolution, and recognising patterns of trends, forecasters are able to provide designers and brands with a ‘vision’ of the future.
