Which disposable items are recycled into a polyester fiber that is being used to make fabric for t-shirts and filling for pillows?

(A) Paper napkins

(B) ** Plastic bottles

(C) Tin cans

(D) Styrofoam plates

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Arora Fibres recycles discarded plastic bottles into polyester used as packaging material Donâ€™t chuck out those plastic bottles that have been piling up in your kitchen for days. They can be re-used to make polyester fabric.

Concept note-2: -Recycled polyester, often called rPet, is made from recycled plastic bottles. It is a great way to divert plastic from our landfills. The production of recycled polyester requires far fewer resources than that of new fibers and generates fewer CO2 emissions.

Concept note-3: -Polyester or recycled polyester is used to make synthetic fabrics, industrial fibers, yarns, and ropes used in everything from car tire reinforcements to conveyer belts.

Concept note-4: -Polyester Yarn is made with used plastic bottles which are ready to be recycled, they are cut, chopped, and melted, then formed into flakes. 100% Recycled Yarn is obtained from these flakes to make recycled fabrics.
