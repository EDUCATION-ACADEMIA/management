(A) customers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Specialization can set small businesses apart from the competition. Specializing in a specific product, service, or clientele provides the greatest opportunity for businesses to become the best at what they do and to deliver continuous innovation around optimizing products or services.

Concept note-2: -A good brand image can help a company establish credibility within its industry. It requires earning the respect of consumers and competitors through quality, consistency and honesty. There are many ways that brands establish credibility, such as: Being honest with consumers.

Concept note-3: -A strong brand image can create credibility and customer loyalty for your company. When a brand is known for delivering what they promise, customers will begin to trust the brand. This can increase customer retention and how often they return.
