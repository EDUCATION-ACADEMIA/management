What is a focal point in visual merchandising?

(A) The items a customer sees as they walk through the door

(B) ** A display used in the centre of the store

(C) A feature in the store window

(D) An area of discounted products

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A focal point is a hotspot that acts as the centrepiece of a merchandising design in combination with styled mannequins or displays, props or signage. These focal points tend to be placed in key sightlines, for example entrances to store departments and are mostly evident in window displays.

Concept note-2: -The focal point reflects your main idea, the thing you most want your audience to see.
