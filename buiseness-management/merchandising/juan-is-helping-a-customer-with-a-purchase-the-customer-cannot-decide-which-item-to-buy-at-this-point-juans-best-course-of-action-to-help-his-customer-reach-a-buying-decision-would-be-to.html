[SOURCES]
Juan is helping a customer with a purchase. The customer cannot decide which item to buy. At this point, Juan’s best course of action to help his customer reach a buying decision would be to:

(A) Show the customer more new merchandise.

(B) ** Stop showing additional items.

(C) Demonstrate another product.

(D) Use the standing-room only close.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Juan is helping a customer with a purchase. The customer cannot decide which item to buy. At this point, Juan’s BEST course of action to help his customer reach a buying decision would be to: Stop showing additional items.

Concept note-2: -If a customer is having difficulty making a buying decision, the salesperson should quit showing them additional merchandise. The standing-room-only close is used when the price of a product will soon increase. The first attempt to get a customer’s agreement to buy is a trial close.
