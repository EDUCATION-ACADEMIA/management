Concept note-1: -COGS is deducted from revenues (sales) in order to calculate gross profit and gross margin. Higher COGS results in lower margins. The value of COGS will change depending on the accounting standards used in the calculation.

Concept note-2: -Cost of goods sold (COGS) is calculated by taking the value of inventory at the beginning of the period being studied, adding the cost of any new inventory purchased over the covered period, and subtracting the value of inventory held at the end of the period.
