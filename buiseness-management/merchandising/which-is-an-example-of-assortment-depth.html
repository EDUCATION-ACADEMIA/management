Which is an example of assortment depth?

(A) ** A fashion retailer carries many dresses in a large range of sizes and colors.

(B) A women’s boutique is known for its selection of accessories and clothing.

(C) Alex’s Boutique carries many different types of men’s suits.

(D) Bloomingdale’s department store offers many different types, brands, and price ranges of dresses.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If the warehouse and the retail trade have a material group in different variations, this is referred to as a deep assortment. The best examples of this are a bakery or a fish shop. The many kinds of bread or fish make the mixture a deep offer.

Concept note-2: -101) Category killers are giant specialty stores that carry a very deep assortment of a particular line.

Concept note-3: –Specialty stores carry narrow product lines with deep assortments within those lines.-Convenience stores are small stores that carry a limited line of high-turnover convenience goods.

Concept note-4: –**Variety-the number of merchandise categories a retailer offers. Often referred to as the breadth of merchandise.-Assortment-the number of different items in a merchandise category.
