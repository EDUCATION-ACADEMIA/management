Which would MOST LIKELY accelerate the fashion movement?

(A) ** Angela’s promotion to a job with more administrative responsibility and higher pay

(B) Carla’s confirmation into a religious group that discourages personal adornment

(C) Government regulations that restrict the use of synthetic fibers in clothing

(D) Military conflict between the United States and China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Good economic conditions: Accelerate fashion movement. During the rise stage of the fashion cycle: Retail fashion buyers order the items in quantity.

Concept note-2: -Who encourages fashion movement? Everyone, from the original designer to the final consumer encourages fashion movement. If most fashion items do not wear out, why do consumers by more clothes? How many fashion trends can exist at the same time?

Concept note-3: -What is the Meaning of Fashion Movement? A fashion movement refers to the dissemination of a particular type of fashion. For example, if a fashion originated in Mumbai, then from Mumbai it goes to Pune, Bengaluru, Delhi, Hyderabad, etc. Likewise, one type of fashion style moves from one place to another.

Concept note-4: -There are three theories that explain how a fashion may travel to mass acceptance: Trickle-down theory. Trickle-up theory. Trickle-across theory.
