A realistic setting depicts a room, area or ____ locale.

(A) distant

(B) ** recognizable

(C) new

(D) innovative

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Characteristics of Realistic Fiction Setting is an accurate portrayal of the world as we know it. The setting could exist; it is a vivid and accurate depiction. It contains believable stories as the writer uses everyday occurrences and/or extreme realism; the story is not true but it could be.

Concept note-2: -Students are familiar with the four types of setting: physical, social, historical and psychological.

Concept note-3: -The three types of setting are the elements of time, place, and environment (both physical and social). Each of these types contributes to building the setting of a story.

Concept note-4: -Local color or regional literature is fiction and poetry that focuses on the characters, dialect, customs, topography, and other features particular to a specific region.
