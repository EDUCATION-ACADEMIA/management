money paid regularly at a certain rate

(A) maintenance

(B) disadvantage

(C) ** interest

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Simple (Regular) Interest Simple or regular interest is the amount of interest due on the loan, based on the principal loan outstanding.

Concept note-2: -Interest: Money paid regularly at a particular rate for the use of money lent. Interest Rate: The proportion of a loan that is charged as interest to the borrower, typically expressed as an annual percentage of the loan outstanding.

Concept note-3: -Interest is money paid regularly at a particular rate for the use of money lent, or for delaying the repayment of debt.

Concept note-4: -There are essentially three main types of interest rates: the nominal interest rate, the effective rate, and the real interest rate. The nominal interest of an investment or loan is simply the stated rate on which interest payments are calculated.
