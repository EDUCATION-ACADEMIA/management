Which answer below is an example of earned income?

(A) Interest earned on a savings account.

(B) ** Money earned from working at the mall.

(C) Money received from Social Security

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Examples of earned income are: wages; salaries; tips; and other taxable employee compensation. Earned income also includes net earnings from self-employment.

Concept note-2: -Earned income Earned income includes wages, salaries, tips, and self-employment earnings you get from working.

Concept note-3: -Earned income includes all the taxable income and wages you get from working for someone else, yourself or from a business or farm you own.

Concept note-4: -Unearned Income. Unearned income includes investment-type income such as taxable interest, ordinary dividends, and capital gain distributions. It also includes unemployment compensation, taxable social security benefits, pensions, annuities, cancellation of debt, and distributions of unearned income from a trust.
