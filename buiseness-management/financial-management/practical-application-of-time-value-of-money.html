EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -TVM can be used to identify the future amount or to identify the present value of the future amount. Therefore, TVM plays a crucial role in not just investment decisions but also financial decisions. For example, assume that an individual has the opportunity to receive $1, 000 today or a year later.

Concept note-2: -The applications of the time value of money may involve loan valuation, bonds valuation, capital budgeting decisions, investment analysis, and personal finance analysis.

Concept note-3: -Time value of money real life example, if you put $100 in a bank, you may be willing to accept a $5 return on an investment after a year. This is because the risk that the bank will not repay you is low. If you lend the same $100 to a stranger, you may require a $20 return on investment instead.
