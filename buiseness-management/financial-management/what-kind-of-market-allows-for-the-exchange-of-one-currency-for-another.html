What kind of market allows for the exchange of one currency for another?

(A) foreign investment market

(B) ** foreign exchange market

(C) internet trading

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The foreign exchange market, also known as the forex market, is a global marketplace for trading in currencies. It is a decentralised market that allows you to buy and sell foreign exchange. The market is an over-the-counter market and the foreign exchange rates will be dictated by it.

Concept note-2: -foreign exchange market (forex, or FX, market), institution for the exchange of one countryâ€™s currency with that of another country.

Concept note-3: -The foreign exchange market is an over-the-counter (OTC) marketplace that determines the exchange rate for global currencies. It is, by far, the largest financial market in the world and is comprised of a global network of financial centers that transact 24 hours a day, closing only on the weekends.

Concept note-4: -More specifically, foreign exchange transactions include: the spot foreign exchange purchase and sale and foreign exchange transaction business for personal customers; the spot, forward and option-date forward foreign exchange transaction business for corporate customers; and the foreign currency option transaction.
