Nancy’s 50-meter freestyle swim time was 28.004 seconds. Betty’s 50-meter freestyle swim time was 27.949 seconds. What was the difference between Nancy’s and Betty’s swim times?

(A) 0.088 second

(B) ** 0.055 second

(C) 0.099 second

(D) 0.801 second

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We have to calculate the difference between their swim times. So to find difference from Nancy’s swim time and Betty’s swim time we will subtract Betty’s swim time from Nancy’s time. The difference will be = 28.004-27.949 = 0.055 sec. And the winner is Betty who took 0.055 seconds lesser than Nancy’s swim time.
