Concept note-1: -What Is a Commercial Bank? The term “commercial bank” refers to a financial institution that accepts deposits, offers checking account services, makes various loans, and offers basic financial products like certificates of deposit (CDs) and savings accounts to individuals and small businesses.

Concept note-2: -YOU ARE PART OWNER. Credit unions are owned and controlled by the people, or members, who use their services.
