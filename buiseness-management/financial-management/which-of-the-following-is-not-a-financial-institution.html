Which of the following is not a financial institution?

(A) A pension fund

(B) ** A newspaper publisher

(C) A commercial bank

(D) An insurance company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -from the given options only Pension Fund is not a financial institution. a newspaper publisher is a financial institution because it is drawn to earn some amount of profit. A commercial bank is a financial institution that lends money to the public and also borrows from them in the name of savings.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is BCCI. BCCI is not a financial institution.

Concept note-3: -Central banks, commercial banks, investment entities, credit unions, thrift institutions, insurance companies, etc., are some of the widely available financial institution types. They also offer consultation services to consumers who seek advice on the pros and cons of making a particular investment.

Concept note-4: -Non-banking financial institutions include insurance companies, investment banks, and mutual funds such as the SBI Mutual Fund. Q. Which of the following is a non-banking financial institution?
