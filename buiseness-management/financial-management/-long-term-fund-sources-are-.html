____ Long term fund sources are ____

(A) Retained earnings

(B) Debentures

(C) Share capital

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Long term fund sources are Retained earnings, Debentures and Share capital. A long/short fund is a type of mutual fund that takes long and short positions in investments typically from a specific market segment.

Concept note-2: -Commercial papers is not a source of long-term finance.

Concept note-3: -Long-term finance can be defined as any financial instrument with maturity exceeding one year (such as bank loans, bonds, leasing and other forms of debt finance), and public and private equity instruments.

Concept note-4: -The types are: 1. Equity Shares 2. Preference Shares 3. Debentures.

Concept note-5: -Meaning:-The. Sources of Long Term Finance are those sources from where the funds are raised for a longer period of time, usually more than a year. Long term financing is required for modernization, expansion, diversification and development of business operations. Long Term Sources of Finance.
