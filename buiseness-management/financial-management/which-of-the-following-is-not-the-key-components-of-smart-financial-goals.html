Which of the following is not the key components of SMART financial goals?

(A) ** Salary

(B) Measurable

(C) Attainable

(D) Realistic

(E) Time-bounded

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are five essential components of a financial plan such as Insurance planning, Retirement Planning, Investment Planning, Tax Planning and Estate Planning.

Concept note-2: -One easy way to help identify your financial goals is to use the acronym “SMART” (specific, measurable, attainable, relevant, timely) to help you create and pursue actionable, realistic goals.

Concept note-3: -Ensuring excess availability of funds at the right time is not an objective of financial planning.
