A(n) ____ is the purchase of goods that are not consumed today but are used in the future to create wealth

(A) Credit

(B) ** Investment

(C) Income

(D) Savings

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An investment is an asset or item acquired with the goal of generating income or appreciation. Appreciation refers to an increase in the value of an asset over time. When an individual purchases a good as an investment, the intent is not to consume the good but rather to use it in the future to create wealth.

Concept note-2: -In an economic outlook, an investment is the purchase of goods that are not consumed today but are used in the future to generate wealth.

Concept note-3: -Stocks, real estate, and precious metals are all ownership investments.
