A $100 deposit today that earns an annual interest rate of 10% is worth how much at the end of two years? Assume all interest received at the end of the first year is reinvested the second year.

(A) ** $121

(B) $120

(C) $141

(D) $122

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: If the Interest Rate is 10 Percent, then the Future Value in Two Years of $100 Today is $120.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The calculated present value of $100 to be paid in 2 years is $82.64.

Concept note-3: -Present value is the value today of an amount of money in the future. If the appropriate interest rate is 10 percent, then the present value of $100 spent or earned one year from now is $100 divided by 1.10, which is about $91.

Concept note-4: -Answer and Explanation: The $100 investment becomes $161.05 after 5 years at 10% compound interest.
