____ is money given for a period of time but must be paid back with interest

(A) Debt

(B) ** Loan

(C) Income

(D) Taxes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A loan is a commitment that you (the borrower) will receive money from a lender, and you will pay back the total borrowed, with added interest, over a defined time period. The terms of each loan are defined in a contract provided by the lender.

Concept note-2: -A loan is when money is given to another party in exchange for repayment of the loan principal amount plus interest.

Concept note-3: -Repayment is the act of paying back money previously borrowed from a lender. Typically, the return of funds happens through periodic payments, which include both principal and interest. The principal refers to the original sum of money borrowed in a loan.

Concept note-4: -Interest payments are the cost of borrowing money. The borrower makes these payments in addition to paying back the principal on a loan. If you lend money with interest, the interest payment is the amount you are paid over and above the principal amount you lent.
