Which of the following legal forms of organization is most expensive to organize?

(A) Sole proprietorships.

(B) Partnerships.

(C) ** Corporations.

(D) Limited partnership.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -a) option c is correct. Corporations legal forms of organisation is most expensive to organize.

Concept note-2: -The three major forms of business in the United States are sole proprietorships, partnerships, and corporations. Each form has implications for how individuals are taxed and resources are managed and deployed.

Concept note-3: -These include corporations, cooperatives, partnerships, sole traders, limited liability company and other specifically labelled types of entities.
