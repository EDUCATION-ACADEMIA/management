This type of policy protects against financial loss arising from negligence in providing professional services.

(A) ** Malpractice Insurance

(B) Health Insurance

(C) Bonding

(D) Employee Life Insurance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Professional Liability Insurance Policy covers all the legal liabilities arising out of negligence, omissions, or errors of any professional whilst rendering service that causes damage to the property of third-party or injury to the third-party itself.

Concept note-2: -Liability insurance is the type of policy that protects the insured against financial liabilities resulting from injuries to any person as well as property damage. Apart from this, liability insurance provides coverage for any legal cost or debt that the responsible party is found legally guilty of.

Concept note-3: -Errors and Omission (E&O) insurance falls under the category of professional liability insurance (PLI). It protects companies and employees who provide professional services from the claims of negligent actions or inadequate work from clients.

Concept note-4: -Professional Liability Insurance (PLI), is also known as Professional Indemnity Insurance (PII) or as Errors & Omissions (E&O). This is a liability insurance cover that protects individuals from professional risks and related legal expenses.

Concept note-5: -The two most common types of indemnity insurance include malpractice insurance and errors & omissions insurance.
