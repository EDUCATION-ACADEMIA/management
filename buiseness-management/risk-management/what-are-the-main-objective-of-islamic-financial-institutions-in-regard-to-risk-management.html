What Are the Main Objective of Islamic Financial Institutions in Regard to Risk Management?

(A) ** To Maximise Profit and Shareholder Value by Providing Different Financial Services Mainly by Managing Risks Within Shariah Parameter.

(B) To Understand the Risk or Return Trade-Off.

(C) To Utilising Financial Products to Hedge Against Risk (Such as Using Options, Futures, Insurance).

(D) To Have Proper Valuation Methodologies to Assess Quality of Investments and Determine the Allocations and Expected Returns.
