How many steps are there in the Risk Management Process?

(A) 1

(B) 3

(C) ** 5

(D) 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The five steps of the risk management process are identification, assessment, mitigation, monitoring, and reporting risks. By following the steps outlined below, you will be able to create a basic risk management plan for your business.

Concept note-2: -There are five basic steps that are taken to manage risk; these steps are referred to as the risk management process. It begins with identifying risks, goes on to analyze risks, then the risk is prioritized, a solution is implemented, and finally, the risk is monitored.

Concept note-3: -The 4 essential steps of the Risk Management Process are: Identify the risk. Assess the risk. Treat the risk. Monitor and Report on the risk.
