When is the risk and uncertainty in a project’s life cycle at the highest?

(A) ** start

(B) design

(C) closing

(D) implementation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Level of uncertainty (risk of failing) and Stakeholders; Influence is: highest at the start of the project.

Concept note-2: -Explanation. Stakeholder influences, risks and uncertainty are the highest at the start of the project.

Concept note-3: -So the level of uncertainty and risk is highest during the Conceptual and Development Phases and, as more information about the project is known, it will gradually be reduced through the Execution Phase.

Concept note-4: -Project risk is highest during the: concept stage of the project life cycle. The period of highest risk impact for a project is in the: Concept stage of the project life cycle.

Concept note-5: -When to Identify Risks. The risk has to be identified at all stages of the project, as it’s an iterative process, but the risks are always greatest at the beginning of projects. At the beginning of the project, the uncertainty is high because of a lack of information at the beginning of the project.
