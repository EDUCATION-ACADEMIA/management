Wanda is working with one of her organization’s European Union business partners to facil-itate the exchange of customer information. Wanda’s organization is located in the United States. What would be the best method for Wanda to use to ensure GDPR compliance?

(A) Binding corporate rules

(B) Privacy Shield

(C) ** Standard contractual clauses

(D) Safe harbor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The GDPR does apply outside Europe The law, therefore, applies to organizations that handle such data whether they are EU-based organizations or not, known as “extra-territorial effect.”

Concept note-2: -If personal data is collected or otherwise processed in the context of the activities of any establishment in the EEA or UK, then the personal data is protected by that area’s GDPR, even if the processing occurs outside the EEA or the UK.

Concept note-3: -Does the GDPR Apply Outside Europe? The whole point of GDPR Certification is to protect the personal data of EU citizens and residents. So the answer is yes-GDPR applies to any cloud-hosted company that collects and uses EU citizens’ data regardless of whether the company is EU-based.

Concept note-4: -The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is legislation that updated and unified data privacy laws across the European Union (EU). GDPR was approved by the European Parliament on April 14, 2016 and went into effect on May 25, 2018. GDPR replaces the EU Data Protection Directive of 1995.
