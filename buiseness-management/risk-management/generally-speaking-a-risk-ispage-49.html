Generally speaking, a risk is:Page 49

(A) anything that results in injury.

(B) anything that might result in injury.

(C) ** anything bad that could happen.

(D) a gamble.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Risk Types: The different types of risks are categorized in several different ways. Risks are classified into some categories, including market risk, credit risk, operational risk, strategic risk, liquidity risk, and event risk. Financial risk is one of the high-priority risk types for every business.

Concept note-2: -In simple terms, risk is the possibility of something bad happening. Risk involves uncertainty about the effects/implications of an activity with respect to something that humans value (such as health, well-being, wealth, property or the environment), often focusing on negative, undesirable consequences.

Concept note-3: -There is an important distinction to be made as some risks can actually pose a benefit to any company while others cause a greater reason for concern. Without risk, it can become easy to settle into consistency, security and stability.
