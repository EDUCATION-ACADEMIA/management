Concept note-1: -ISO Guide 73:2009 is intended to be used by: those engaged in managing risks, those who are involved in activities of ISO and IEC, and. developers of national or sector-specific standards, guides, procedures and codes of practice relating to the management of risk.

Concept note-2: -ISO 31000, Risk management – Guidelines, provides principles, a framework and a process for managing risk.

Concept note-3: -3, ISO 27001 notes that information security management in ISO 27001 is aligned with ISO 31000. Therefore, ISO 27001 does not say you need to implement risk assessment and treatment according to ISO 31000 – it only says that all the requirements from ISO 27001 are already compliant with ISO 31000.
