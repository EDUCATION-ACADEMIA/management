(A) Auto Insurance

(B) Health Insurance

(C) Home Insurance

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An income statement is a financial statement that shows you the company’s income and expenditures. It also shows whether a company is making profit or loss for a given period.

Concept note-2: -Pay yourself first (PYF) means automatically setting aside money from each paycheck, as soon as you receive it, rather than waiting to see what, if anything, is left over to save at the end of the month.

Concept note-3: -The largest expense for most Americans is housing. At $1, 050 per month, the cost of having a roof over our heads accounts for 21% of a household’s monthly budget. Percentage of income is based on after-tax income.

Concept note-4: -Gross pay is what employees earn before taxes, benefits and other payroll deductions are withheld from their wages. The amount remaining after all withholdings are accounted for is net pay or take-home pay.
