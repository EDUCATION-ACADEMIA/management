Which one IS NOT the contains of Risk Register:

(A) Risk Identified

(B) Level of Risk

(C) ** Achievement of KPI

(D) Management Appetite

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Most CEOs, CFOs, and financial analysts will tell you that revenue is a KPI (it’s really not), second only to profit (which is also not a KPI).

Concept note-2: -What are KPIs for risk management? KPIs, or key performance indicators, for risk management are metrics for assessing risks for a business. KPIs evaluate the critical parts of a business that it needs for it to be successful in meeting its objectives.

Concept note-3: -Types of KPIs Quantitative indicators that can be presented with a number. Qualitative indicators that can’t be presented as a number. Leading indicators that can predict the outcome of a process.
