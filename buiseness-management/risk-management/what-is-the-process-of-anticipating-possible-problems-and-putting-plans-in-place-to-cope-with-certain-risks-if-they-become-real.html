What is the process of anticipating possible problems and putting plans in place to cope with certain risks if they become real?

(A) Risk adjustment

(B) ** Risk management

(C) Risk tolerance

(D) Risk abatement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Risk Analysis is a process that helps you to identify and manage potential problems that could undermine key business initiatives or projects. However, it can also be applied to other projects outside of business, such as organizing events or even buying a home!

Concept note-2: -There are five basic steps that are taken to manage risk; these steps are referred to as the risk management process. It begins with identifying risks, goes on to analyze risks, then the risk is prioritized, a solution is implemented, and finally, the risk is monitored.

Concept note-3: -In business, risk management is defined as the process of identifying, monitoring and managing potential risks in order to minimize the negative impact they may have on an organization.
