A type of insurance that protects the owner of a business from loss when employees or customers are injured on the premises is called liability insurance.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If a third party sustains any injury, damage or death due to the services offered by the professionals, the possibility of a lawsuit is higher. To bear those legal expenses, Professional Liability comes in handy.

Concept note-2: -Liability insurance is a policy that offers protection to businesses and individuals from risk that they may be held legally or sued for negligence, malpractice or injury. This insurance policy protects the insured from legal payouts and costs for which the policyholder is deemed to be responsible.

Concept note-3: -Liability insurance is the type of policy that protects the insured against financial liabilities resulting from injuries to any person as well as property damage. Apart from this, liability insurance provides coverage for any legal cost or debt that the responsible party is found legally guilty of.

Concept note-4: -Different types of general insurance include motor insurance, health insurance, travel insurance, and home insurance.

Concept note-5: -Personal Accident Insurance-These medical insurance policies only cover financial liability from injuries, disability or death arising due to accidents.
