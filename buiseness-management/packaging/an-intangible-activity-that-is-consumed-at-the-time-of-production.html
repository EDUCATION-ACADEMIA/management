An intangible activity that is consumed at the time of production

(A) Product

(B) Slogan

(C) ** Service

(D) Heterogeneous

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Consumer services are intangible products or actions that are typically produced and consumed simultaneously.

Concept note-2: -Examples of intangible services include health care, physical security, and legal services from lawyers.

Concept note-3: -Services are intangible because they can often not be seen, tasted, felt, heard, or smelled before they are purchased. A person purchasing plastic surgery cannot see the results before the purchase, and a lawyer’s client cannot anticipate the outcome of a case before the lawyer’s work is presented in court.

Concept note-4: -Service products are consumed at the same time they are being produced.
