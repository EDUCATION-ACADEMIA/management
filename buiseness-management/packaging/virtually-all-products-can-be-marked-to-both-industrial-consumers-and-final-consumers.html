Virtually all products can be marked to both industrial consumers and final consumers.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Products are broadly classified into two categories – consumer products and industrial products. Consumer products are products that the ultimate consumer purchases himself for direct use. The consumer purchases these consumer products to satisfy his personal needs and desires.

Concept note-2: -Industrial goods are bought and used for industrial and business use. Consumer goods are ready for the consumption and satisfaction of human wants. While industrial goods are made up of machinery, plants, and raw materials, consumer goods are commodities purchased by a buyer like clothing, food, and drinks.

Concept note-3: -Types of Products – 3 Main Types: Consumer Products, Industrial Products and Services. There are a number of useful ways of classifying products. One of the most basic way was the different ways of making a journey.

Concept note-4: -There are four types of products and each is classified based on consumer habits, price, and product characteristics: convenience goods, shopping goods, specialty products, and unsought goods.
