During which step of the new product development process do companies focus on characteristics like market size, marketing impact, and effect on the company image?

(A) ** Idea screening

(B) Idea generation

(C) Product testing

(D) Customer acceptance/evaluation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Philip Kotler says, “Test marketing is the stage at which the product and marketing programs are introduced into more realistic market settings.”

Concept note-2: -Commercialization The final stage of your new product development process is commercialization, where you introduce your products to market.

Concept note-3: -Evaluation, not generation or development. Test marketing is the last step in a process whose goal is a successful product. The “ideal” process begins with the generation of new product ideas and ends with the evaluation of a complete marketing plan.

Concept note-4: -Idea generation During product discovery, companies examine market trends, conduct research, and dig deep into users’ wants and needs to identify a problem and propose innovative solutions.
