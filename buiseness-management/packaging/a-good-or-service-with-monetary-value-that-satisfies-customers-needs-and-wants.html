A good or service with monetary value that satisfies customers’ needs and wants.

(A) Slogan

(B) ** Product

(C) Headline

(D) Intangible

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is a series of activities fashion businesses undertake so that customers will buy products from them instead of their competitors. What is Marketing Concept? The idea that businesses must satisfy customers’ needs and wants in order to make a profit.

Concept note-2: -Goods are tangible items that have monetary value and satisfy customers’ needs and wants. Examples of economic goods are cars, furniture, electronics, and clothing. Services are intangible items that have monetary value and satisfy customers’ needs and wants.

Concept note-3: -Define “Marketing”. The process of developing, promoting, and distributing products, or goods and services, to satisfy customers’ needs and wants. Goods are tangible items such as sports equipment. That is, you can touch and feel them. Services are intangible products, such as going to a movie.

Concept note-4: -Product definition: A product is a good, service, or idea consisting of a bundle of tangible and intangible attributes that satisfies consumers and is received in exchange for money or some other unit of value.
