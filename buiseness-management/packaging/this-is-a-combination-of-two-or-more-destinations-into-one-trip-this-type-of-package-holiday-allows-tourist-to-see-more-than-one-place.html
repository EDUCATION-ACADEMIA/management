PACKAGING

[SOURCES]
This is a combination of two or more destinations into one trip. This type of package holiday allows tourist to see more than one place

(A) Static Holiday

(B) All Inclusive Holiday

(C) ** Multi Center Holiday

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Package tours are generally classified on the basis of their operation and types of services included. These are basically classified into independent tour, escorted tour, incentive tour, hosted tour and freedom tour.

Concept note-2: -(Last Updated On: July 13, 2022) Multi-destination holidays involve splitting your holiday time between two or more destinations. This could be several areas of the same country, staying for a few days in each. Or it could mean visiting more than one country on the same trip.

Concept note-3: -Inbound tourism refers to the activities of a visitor from outside of country of residence (e.g. a Spaniard visiting Britain). Outbound tourism refers to the activities of a resident visitor outside of their country of residence (e.g. a Brit visiting an overseas country).
