Which option below refers to the use of questionnaires, directly asking customers or potential consumers about their opinions and preferences?

(A) focus group

(B) interviews

(C) academic journals

(D) ** surveys

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -While there isn’t an official book of survey questions or survey taxonomy, I find it helpful to break down survey questions into four classes: open-ended, closed-ended (static), closed-ended (dynamic), and task-based.

Concept note-2: -A customer satisfaction survey is a questionnaire designed to help businesses understand what their customers think about their products or services, their brand, and their customer support.

Concept note-3: -Questionnaire surveys are a technique for gathering statistical information about the attributes, attitudes, or actions of a population by a structured set of questions.

Concept note-4: -Ranking questions A ranking question asks respondents to order answer choices by way of preference. This allows you to not only understand how respondents feel about each answer option, but it also helps you understand each one’s relative popularity.
