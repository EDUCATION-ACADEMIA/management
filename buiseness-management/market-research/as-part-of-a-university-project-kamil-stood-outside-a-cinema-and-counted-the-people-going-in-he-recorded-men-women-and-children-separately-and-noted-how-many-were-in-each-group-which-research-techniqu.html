As part of a university project, Kamil stood outside a cinema and counted the people going in. He recorded men, women and children separately and noted how many were in each group. Which research technique was he using?

(A) survey

(B) focus group

(C) ** observation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Quantitative research typically requires much larger numbers of respondents than qualitative research. The main job of a moderator is to describe the responses in an organized written report, not interpret or explain them. Secondary research information adds most value when used on its own separate from primary data.

Concept note-2: -Exploratory Research: This research is conducted to explore information about the nature or causes of research problem. It is conducted when the causes of the research problem are not known to the researcher. Descriptive Research: Descriptive research, also known as”Statistical research”.

Concept note-3: -Unstructured informal interview Such interviews are entirely informal and are not controlled by a specific set of detailed questions. Rather the interviewer is guided by a pre-defined list of issues.

Concept note-4: -15. What is it called when research agencies use a single questionnaire to collect primary data on behalf of a number of their business clients at the same time? postal survey.
