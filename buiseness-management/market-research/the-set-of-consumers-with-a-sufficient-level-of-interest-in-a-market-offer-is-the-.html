The set of consumers with a sufficient level of interest in a market offer is the ____

(A) total market

(B) available market

(C) ** potential market

(D) penetrated market

(E) target market

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The set of consumers with a sufficient level ofinterestin a market offer. Target Market: Available Market

AvailableMarket = Population ∗ ( Interested ) ∗ [ ( Incomelevel ) ∗ ( Access ) ]

The set of consumers who haveinterest, incomeandaccessto a particular offer.

Concept note-2: -The potential market or market potential is the maximum volume of sales – in physical units or monetary terms – that could be available to all companies in a given industry over a given period. Factors such as a specific level of marketing expenditure or environmental conditions must also be taken into account.

Concept note-3: -primary consumers, secondary consumers, tertiary consumers or apex consumers are the different types of consumers.

Concept note-4: -Market potential is the valuation of the sales revenue from all the supplying channels in a market. The potential of the market is the population who is interested in the product or service which is being made or offered by an organisation.
