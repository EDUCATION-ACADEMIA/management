What is an example of primary research?

(A) Magazine

(B) ** Observation

(C) Newspaper

(D) Government statistics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Primary research is any type of research that you collect yourself. Examples include surveys, interviews, observations, and ethnographic research.

Concept note-2: -Observations: In this primary research method, there is no direct interaction between researcher and person/consumer being observed. Researcher observes the reactions of a subject and makes notes.

Concept note-3: -Examples of primary sources: Autobiographies and memoirs. Diaries, personal letters, and correspondence. Interviews, surveys, and fieldwork.

Concept note-4: -Examples of primary research data are student thesis, market research and first-person accounts of trauma survivors while examples of secondary research data include newspapers, books, academic journals and magazines.

Concept note-5: -Types of primary research The most common primary market research methods are interviews, surveys, focus groups and observations.
