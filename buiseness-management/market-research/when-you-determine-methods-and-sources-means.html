EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Approach In Quantitative Research, researchers tend to remain objectively separated from the subject matter. This is because Quantitative Research is objective in approach in the sense that it only seeks precise measurements and analysis of target concepts to answer his inquiry.

Concept note-2: -The main method for cause-effect research is experimentation. In experimental-based research, we manipulate the causal or independent variables in a relatively controlled environment. This means that we control and monitor other variables affecting the dependent variable (e.g. sales) as much as possible.

Concept note-3: -While there are a number of different research techniques available, the experimental method is one that allows researchers to look at cause-and-effect relationships.
