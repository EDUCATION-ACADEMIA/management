MARKET RESEARCH

[SOURCES]
Gathering information about your competition’s products, marketing activities, and strengths and weaknesses won’t help your business be more competitive.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Look at your competitors’ career sites, LinkedIn, Yelp, social media channels, Google reviews, and Glassdoor pages to understand more about their inner workings.

Concept note-2: -Knowing who your competitors are, and what they are offering, can help you to make your products, services and marketing stand out. It will enable you to set your prices competitively and help you to respond to rival marketing campaigns with your own initiatives.
