Using a keyword search tool to see what people are searching for is an example of what type of data source?

(A) public data

(B) ** secondary data

(C) reviews

(D) focus group

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Secondary data is information collected from public sources such as websites, periodicals or other literature. When gathering secondary data, it’s important to verify the reliability of a source before using its information.

Concept note-2: -1 – Define research needs and establish goals. 2 – Choose the best sources of secondary market research. 3 – Access, collate, and verify research data. 4 – Analyze, compare, and identify trends.

Concept note-3: -Which of the following best defines the term secondary data? It is data previously collected for any purpose other than the one at hand.

Concept note-4: -Why do many researchers and entrepreneurs use secondary data first? They use secondary data first because its easier and less time consuming to just use the data already collected.
