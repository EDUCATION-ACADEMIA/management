A women’s clothing designer is considering launching a new dress design using a new fabric made from recycled water bottles. It wants to interview 100 customers from its last launch to get feedback on the new design before moving forward with wide scale production. What type of sampling is being used?

(A) Cluster

(B) ** Quota

(C) Convenience

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fast fashion is the term used to describe clothing designs that move quickly from the catwalk to stores to take advantage of trends.

Concept note-2: -Emphasis (Focal Point or Center of Attraction) This essential element of design in fashion draws attention to a specific part of the body or garment. This is done in various ways, such as using different sizes, colors, textures, shapes, etc.

Concept note-3: -Texture refers to the surface interest of a fabric, created by the weave and by light touch–how something feels, or looks like it would feel, when touched. Guidelines for combining design elements.
