Which of the following describes a specific group of consumers to whom a company aims the selling of its products or services?

(A) Customer Base

(B) Aim Customer

(C) Dream Customer

(D) ** Target Customer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A target market is a specific group of people with shared characteristics that a business markets its products or services to. Companies use target markets to thoroughly understand their potential customers and craft marketing strategies that help them meet their business and marketing objectives.

Concept note-2: -A target market is the set of consumers that a company plans to sell to or reach with marketing activities.

Concept note-3: -A target market is a group of potential customers that you identify to sell products or services to. Each group can be divided into smaller segments. Segments are typically grouped by age, location, income and lifestyle.

Concept note-4: -The four target markets are geographic, demographic, psychographic, and behavioral.
