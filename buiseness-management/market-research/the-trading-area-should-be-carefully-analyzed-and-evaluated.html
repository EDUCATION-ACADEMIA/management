EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Four common types of market research techniques include surveys, interviews, focus groups, and customer observation.

Concept note-2: -Some of the overarching goals that market research can help organizations accomplish, include: making important business decisions, securing investments and funding, determining new business opportunities, and even avoiding business failures.

Concept note-3: -Why Do Market Research? Market research provides critical information about your market and your business landscape. It can tell you how your company is perceived by the target customers and clients you want to reach.
