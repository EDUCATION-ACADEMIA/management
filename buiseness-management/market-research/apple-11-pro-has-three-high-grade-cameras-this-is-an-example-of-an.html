Apple 11 Pro has three high grade cameras. This is an example of a(n)

(A) ** improvement

(B) innovation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The major new feature in the iPhone 11 was an upgraded dual-lens camera system. It includes an ƒ/1.8 6-element 12-megapixel wide-angle lens (26mm focal length) and an ƒ/2.4 5-element 12-megapixel ultra wide-angle lens (13mm focal length), up from a single 12-megapixel camera lens in the iPhone XR.

Concept note-2: -The iPhone 14 Pro has three rear cameras: a main wide-angle camera, an ultrawide and a telephoto camera with 3x optical zoom. The lens on the main camera has a wider focal length, going from a 26mm equivalent on previous iPhone models to a 24mm one.

Concept note-3: -First, let’s recap the camera hardware found on the new iPhones: 12MP Standard wide-angle camera, 26mm equivalent, f/1.8 aperture, OIS, and focus pixels. 12MP ultra-wide-angle camera, 13mm-equivalent, f/2.4 lens. 12MP telephoto camera, 52mm-equivalent, f/2 lens and OIS on the Pro and Pro Max.
