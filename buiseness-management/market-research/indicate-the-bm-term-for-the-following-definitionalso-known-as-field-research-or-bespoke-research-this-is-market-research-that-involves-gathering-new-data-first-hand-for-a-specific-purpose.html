Indicate the BM term for the following definition:"Also known as field research or bespoke research, this is market research that involves gathering new data, first-hand, for a specific purpose.”

(A) ** Primary research

(B) Secondary research

(C) Desk research

(D) First hand research

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Market research is the process of gathering and analyzing information about a market, product, or service in order to gain insight into customers, competitors, and the market itself.

Concept note-2: -Market research helps you identify areas for possible business expansion. Given that it offers the opportunity to test markets to determine if there is room for a new product or service. You can also conduct market research to find the best location to open a new store.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The best description of secondary data is a) pieces of information that have been collected prior to the start of the focal research project.

Concept note-4: -Primary research is research you conduct yourself (or hire someone to do for you.) It involves going directly to a source – usually customers and prospective customers in your target market – to ask questions and gather information.
