MARKET RESEARCH

[SOURCES]
Data that helps a business determine where its potential customers live and how far they will travel to visit a business.

(A) Demographics

(B) ** Geographic data

(C) Use-based data

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A trade area defines where customers live and how far they are likely to travel to a particular business or business district.

Concept note-2: -Simply put, customer data is the information you collect about your clients and leads. It can be something as basic as their name and email address or as detailed as behavioral or transactional information.

Concept note-3: -Psychographics are data that help businesses determine how often potential customers use a particular product or service. Spreadsheets and databases are used for collecting and analyzing market research data.

Concept note-4: -Make sure you get their name, gender, age, profession, location, email address, phone number, and household income. This should be enough to develop a basic profile on your customer and get in touch with them when you need to.
