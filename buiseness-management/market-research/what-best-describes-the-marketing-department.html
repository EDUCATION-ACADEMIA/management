What best describes the marketing department?

(A) ** Promotion & Advertising

(B) Helping to sell products

(C) Recording financial information

(D) Conducting background business tasks

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“What is Promotion?” Promotion is a marketing tool, used as a strategy to communicate between the sellers and buyers. Through this, the seller tries to influence and convince the buyers to buy their products or services. It assists in spreading the word about the product or services or company to the people.

Concept note-2: -It serves as the face of your company, coordinating and producing all materials representing the business. It is the Marketing Department’s job to reach out to prospects, customers, investors and/or the community, while creating an overarching image that represents your company in a positive light.

Concept note-3: -Focussing on customer value and satisfaction.

Concept note-4: -A marketing department drives the promotional engine of a business. It is responsible for increasing brand awareness overall, while also driving potential and recurring customers to a company’s products or services.
