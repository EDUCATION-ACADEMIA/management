One of the advantages of reviewing secondary data it can be done quickly and easily.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following is an advantage of secondary data? They often allow researchers to gain exploratory insights.

Concept note-2: -Advantages of Secondary Research Secondary data can be faster and cheaper to obtain, depending on the sources you use. Secondary research can help to: Answer certain research questions and test some hypotheses. Formulate an appropriate research design (e.g., identify key variables).

Concept note-3: -Expert Answer Correct option is (C) It saves time in data collection. Secondary data means that you are using the data that has already been collected by someone else for your own research.
