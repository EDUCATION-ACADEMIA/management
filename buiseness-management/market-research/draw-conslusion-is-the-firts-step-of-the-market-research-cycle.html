MARKET RESEARCH

[SOURCES]
Draw Conslusion is the firts step of the market research cycle

(A) Yes, because you need to identify through a draw your objectives

(B) ** No, because you use the information from the others steps to get conclusions

(C) No, because Draw Conclusion is the 5th step in the market research cycle

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What are the stages of the marketing life cycle? The three key stages are attract, nurture, and conversion. Your path should go from engaging the prospect, to nurturing them at each stage of getting to know your brand to converting them into a brand advocate buyer.

Concept note-2: -The first step in the marketing research process, then, is to establish a SMART objective. This should be a Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Relevant and Timely marketing goal that the organization wishes to achieve: to develop, to grow, to compare, to measure, etc.

Concept note-3: -Report, Present, Take Action. The final step in the market research process is to present your survey research findings and draw conclusions.
