The overall theory does which of the following?

(A) Describes the goal of the research

(B) ** Establishes the hypothesis to prove or disprove with research

(C) Explains the information uncovered by the research

(D) Lists the methods to be used while performing research

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An Experiment: An experiment is a controlled test designed specifically to prove or disprove a hypothesis.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: Research Hypothesis: The research hypothesis is the statement that is contrary to the null hypothesis. It represents the statement of what the researcher is trying to predict and is also known as the alternative hypothesis.

Concept note-3: -Aspects of Quantitative (Empirical Research) It uses deductive reasoning, where the researcher forms an hypothesis, collects data in an investigation of the problem, and then uses the data from the investigation, after analysis is made and conclusions are shared, to prove the hypotheses not false or false.

Concept note-4: -Grounded theory describes a qualitative research approach to inductively build a “theory”-that is, it aims to generate testable knowledge from data rather than to test existing knowledge [1].
