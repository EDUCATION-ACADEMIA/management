[SOURCES]
Determining what data to collect and track is the ____ step in designing an electronic record keeping system.

(A) ** first

(B) second

(C) third

(D) final

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To select the right collection method, you’ll need to consider the type of information you want to collect, the timeframe over which you’ll obtain it and the other aspects you determined.

Concept note-2: -Data collection takes place in three steps: In the first step, you create a data collection plan: When do you need what data? In the second step, you identify the appropriate data sources. In the third step, you actually collect the data.

Concept note-3: -Data collection techniques can include: Observations: collecting data without asking questions. Questionnaires: Ask questions among a group of people. Focus Groups: Interviewing and observing a group of people. Documents and Records: old fashion research.
