Sentence in a business plan that describes the purpose of the business.

(A) ** mission statement

(B) market research

(C) Pro forma

(D) planning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The mission statement explains what your business offers, how it does this and who it helps. It captures the overall value of your product or service. If your business follows its mission, it will achieve its vision. As you write your mission statement, remember to make it clear, short and specific.

Concept note-2: -We intend to make enough profit to generate a fair return for our investors and to finance continued growth and development in quality products. We also maintain a friendly, fair, and creative work environment, which respects diversity, new ideas, and hard work.

Concept note-3: -A company mission statement is an action-based statement that declares the purpose of an organization and how they serve their customers. This sometimes includes a description of the company, what it does, and its objectives.
