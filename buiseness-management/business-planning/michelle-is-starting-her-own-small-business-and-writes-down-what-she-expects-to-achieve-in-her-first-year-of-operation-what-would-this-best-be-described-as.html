BUSINESS PLANNING

[SOURCES]
Michelle is starting her own small business and writes down what she expects to achieve in her first year of operation. What would this best be described as?

(A) ** Goal

(B) Mission

(C) Objective

(D) Vision

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Write your business plan Your business plan is the foundation of your business. It’s a roadmap for how to structure, run, and grow your new business. You’ll use it to convince people that working with you-or investing in your company-is a smart choice.

Concept note-2: -Focus on the customer and fully understand the market Research the demographics of your potential customer base and understand their buying habits. Watch competitors, talk with similar businesses, browse your competitors’ websites, and understand what their customers are saying about them on social media.

Concept note-3: -The best way to start a business is to have passion for your project, friends and clients lined up for your business, and start while you still have another job. You’ll need to write a business plan backed by strong research to clarify your vision and attract investors.
