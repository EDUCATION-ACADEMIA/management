Which of the following would be an example of e-commerce?

(A) A business uses the internet to pay their accounts.

(B) ** A business uses the internet to purchase their raw materials online.

(C) A business uses the internet to market their goods and services.

(D) A business has set up an e-mail account to enable them to communicate with their customers and suppliers.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Online stores like Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify, Myntra, Ebay, Quikr, Olx are examples of E-commerce websites.

Concept note-2: -An ecommerce website is any site that allows you to buy and sell products and services online. Companies like Amazon and Alibaba are examples of ecommerce websites.

Concept note-3: -E-commerce is powered by the internet. Customers access an online store to browse through and place orders for products or services via their own devices.
