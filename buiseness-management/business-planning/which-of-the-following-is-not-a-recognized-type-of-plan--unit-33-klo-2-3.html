Which of the following is not a recognized type of plan? ( Unit 33-KLO 2-3)

(A) Business

(B) Succession

(C) ** Ad hoc

(D) Financial

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Ad hoc refers to something that is not planned so it is not recognized as a plan.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The succession plan is not a recognized plan.

Concept note-3: -Types of Plan: Strategy, Policy, Rules, Budgets, Methods with Examples.

Concept note-4: -The four main plans are strategic, tactical, operational, and contingency. The four main plans of business are strategic, tactical, operational and contingency. Strategic planning looks at the long-term issues of the organization, and helps develop a plan for growth or change of business function.
