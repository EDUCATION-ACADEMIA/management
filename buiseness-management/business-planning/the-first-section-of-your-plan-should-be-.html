[SOURCES]
The first section of your plan should be ____

(A) · Marketing

(B) ** Company

(C) Financial

(D) Management

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first section should be a concise overview of your business plan. It should be short, and must be well written. Your goal is to draw readers in so they want to learn more about your company.

Concept note-2: -1. Define your goal in writing. Making an effective action plan starts with defining and documenting the end goal.

Concept note-3: -1. Executive Summary. Your executive summary should appear first in your business plan. It should summarize what you expect your business to accomplish.

Concept note-4: -Write an executive summary This is the first page of your business plan. Think of it as your elevator pitch. It should include a mission statement, a brief description of the products or services offered, and a broad summary of your financial growth plans.

Concept note-5: -Promotion Stage: Promotion is the first step in the formation of a company. In this phase, the idea of starting a business is converted into reality with the help of promoters of the business idea. In this stage the ideas are executed.
