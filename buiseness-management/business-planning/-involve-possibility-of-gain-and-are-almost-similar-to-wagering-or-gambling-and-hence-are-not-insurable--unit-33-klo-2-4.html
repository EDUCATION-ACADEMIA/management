(A) ** Speculative risks

(B) Dynamic risks

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A speculative risk has the potential to result in a gain or a loss.

Concept note-2: -Gambling and investing in the stock market are two examples of speculative risks. Each offers a chance to make money, lose money or walk away even. Again, do not equate gambling and investing on any other level than as both being a speculative risk.

Concept note-3: -Pure risk refers to risks that are beyond human control and result in a loss or no loss with no possibility of financial gain.
