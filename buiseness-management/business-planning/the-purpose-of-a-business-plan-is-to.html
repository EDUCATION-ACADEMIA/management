The purpose of a business plan is to:

(A) Give you business ideas.

(B) Guarantee that you will get capital from investors.

(C) Help you hire employees.

(D) ** Describe the intent or purpose of a new business idea.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -✓ The purpose of a Business Plan is to identify, describe and analyze a business opportunity and/or a business already under way, examining its technical, economic and financial feasibility.

Concept note-2: -A business plan is a written tool about your business that projects 3-5 years ahead and outlines the path your business intends to take to make money and grow revenue. Think of it as a living project for your business, and not as a one-time document.

Concept note-3: -The 3 most important purposes of a business plan are 1) to create an effective strategy for growth, 2) to determine your future financial needs, and 3) to attract investors (including angel investors and VC funding) and lenders.

Concept note-4: -Explanation: The production of a business plan is likely to help (not hinder) the achievement of business aims and objectives and minimise the chance of the business failing. This is because it provides a comprehensive framework which guides operations and decision making.
