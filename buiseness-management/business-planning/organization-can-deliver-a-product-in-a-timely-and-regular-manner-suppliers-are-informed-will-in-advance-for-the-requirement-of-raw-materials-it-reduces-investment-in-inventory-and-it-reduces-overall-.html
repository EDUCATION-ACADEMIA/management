Organization can deliver a product in a timely and regular manner, suppliers are informed will in advance for the requirement of raw materials, it reduces investment in inventory and It reduces overall production cost by driving in efficiency are the steps of ____ ( Unit 33-KLO 2-4)

(A) Procurement controlling

(B) ** Production planning.

(C) Planning.

(D) Controlling.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution: Scheduling is related to the timetable in production planning and control activities.

Concept note-2: -Production Planning and Control aims to ensure cost-efficient and timely manufacturing of goods by coordinating with multiple departments.

Concept note-3: -Routing-Scheduling-Dispatching-Follow up. Scheduling-Routing-Dispatching-Follow up. Dispatching-Routing-Scheduling-Follow up.

Concept note-4: -Three common types of manufacturing production processes are: make to stock (MTS), make to order (MTO), and make to assemble (MTA). Such strategies have advantages and disadvantages in labor costs, inventory control, overhead, customization, and the speed of production and filling orders.
