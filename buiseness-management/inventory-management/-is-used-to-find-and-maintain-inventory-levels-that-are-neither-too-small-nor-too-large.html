____ is used to find and maintain inventory levels that are neither too small nor too large.

(A) Planning purchases

(B) ** Inventory management

(C) Inventory control

(D) Managing purchases

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Inventory management is the entire process of managing inventories from raw materials to finished products.

Concept note-2: -The 4 Types of Inventory Management The types of inventory management are Raw Materials, Works-In-Process, Maintenance, Repair and Operations or MRO and Finished Goods.
