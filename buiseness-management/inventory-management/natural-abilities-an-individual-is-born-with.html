Natural abilities an individual is born with

(A) Laissez-Faire Leadership

(B) Traits

(C) Transformational Leadership

(D) ** Talents

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Everyone is born with inherent abilities that make certain types of tasks preferable to others. For that reason, people are most fulfilled in their careers when they find an environment that allows them to work primarily from their gifts and their natural talents.

Concept note-2: -The science of skill acquisition has been the focus of a number of recent studies and books. As it turns out, we are born with very few, if any, natural talents and skills. Excellence is borne not of any particular innate ability, but of practice.

Concept note-3: -Natural abilities are the traits hardwired within each person that make some tasks in life easier. Career services professionals likely apply this concept to work with clients, but do those individuals get value from simply understanding natural abilities?
