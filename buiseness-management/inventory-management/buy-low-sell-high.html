Buy low, sell high

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Buying low and selling high is generally a good strategy as it allows you to take advantage of price movements in the market. However, there is no guarantee that this strategy will always be successful, and you may end up losing money if the market conditions are not favorable.

Concept note-2: -Key Takeaways. Buy low, sell high is a strategy where you buy stocks or securities at a low price and sell them at a higher price. This strategy can be difficult as prices reflect emotions and psychology and are difficult to predict.

Concept note-3: -Ask any smart investor and they will tell you that the secret to making money in the market is to buy low and sell high.

Concept note-4: -So, buying low and selling high is a very difficult thing to do in real life. This strategy primarily relies on share prices, and the thing is, those prices fluctuate on a regular basis and unless you have a crystal ball, it’s impossible to know where they’ll be heading next. Share prices can depend on many factors.
