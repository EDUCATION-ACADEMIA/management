This method of checking a shipment means that only some items are counted or inspected and that the remaining items are assumed to be there and in the same condition.

(A) Direct Check Method

(B) ** Spot Check Method

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In quality control, there are 4 types of quality inspections, namely: pre-production inspection, during production inspection, pre-shipment inspection, and container loading/unloading inspections. Each of these types of inspection has its own purpose.

Concept note-2: -methods are visual, microscopy, liquid or dye penetrant inspection, mag-netic particle inspection, eddy current testing, x-ray or radiographic test-ing, and ultrasonic testing.
