The SBE purchases products, supplies, and raw materials from businesses that buy in large quantities, store the goods, then resell them in smaller quantities. That type of business is a:

(A) Retailer

(B) ** Wholesaler

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wholesaler is the one who buys goods and services in large quantities at a low unit price to retail merchants and then sells these goods to small traders called retailers.

Concept note-2: -A wholesaler buys products from producers in bulk and sells it in large quantities to traders.

Concept note-3: -The wholesaler is the trader who buy goods in large quantity from various producers. They are the traders who are the first one to buy goods in large quantities from the producer. They then sold the goods to small traders known as retailers.

Concept note-4: -When traders buy goods directly from producers in huge quantities and sell to retailers, it is known as retail trade.

Concept note-5: -Answer: A retailer purchases goods from wholesaler. sells them to the final consumers in relatively smaller amounts.
