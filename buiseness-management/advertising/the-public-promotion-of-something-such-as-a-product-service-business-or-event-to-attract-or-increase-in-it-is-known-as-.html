The public promotion of something such as a product, service, business, or event, to attract or increase in it; is known as: ____

(A) Mass media

(B) Audience

(C) Media planning

(D) ** Advertising

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The definition of advertising is an industry used to call the attention of the public to something, typically a product or service. The definition of advertisement is the means of communication in which a product, brand or service is promoted to a viewership in order to attract interest, engagement, and sales.

Concept note-2: -Advertising is the public promotion of something (product, service, business, or event) to attract (increase) interest in it. Marketing is the whole process to get products out to consumers and advertising are parts of marketing.

Concept note-3: -Advertising-A paid form of communication and promotion involving a product and its attributes.

Concept note-4: -Personal selling is the process of helping and persuading one or more prospects to purchase a good or service or to act on any idea through the use of an oral presentation, often in a face-to-face manner or by telephone.
