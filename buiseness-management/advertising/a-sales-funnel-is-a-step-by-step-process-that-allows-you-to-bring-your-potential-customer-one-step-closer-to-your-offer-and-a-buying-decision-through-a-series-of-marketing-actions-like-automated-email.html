A sales funnel is a step-by-step process that allows you to bring your potential customer one step closer to your offer and a buying decision through a series of marketing actions like automated emails, videos, articles and landing pages that will do the selling for you.

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A sales funnel is a marketing term used to capture and describe the journey that potential customers go through, from prospecting to purchase. A sales funnel consists of several steps, the actual number of which varies with each companyâ€™s sales model.

Concept note-2: -Frequently used by sales and marketing organizations, the sales funnel helps companies understand and visualize their sales process and measure overall conversion success between each step of the funnel. A sales funnel is shaped like an inverted pyramid, similar to real-world funnels, to which the metaphor alludes.

Concept note-3: -What is a sales funnel? A way to see where consumers are in the buying process. A way to generate leads. A way to calculate how much revenue a company will make.
