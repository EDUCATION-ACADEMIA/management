The American Dairy Council’s “Got Milk” advertising campaign has created a favorable impression of milk and generated goodwill toward their product. This is an example of:

(A) Promotional Advertising

(B) Mass Advertising

(C) Communication Advertising

(D) ** Institutional Advertising

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Institutional Advertisements: The objective is to build goodwill or an image for an organization rather than promote a specific good or service.

Concept note-2: -What is the purpose of an institutional advertisement? To build goodwill or an image for an organization rather than a specific offering. This form of advertising is often used to support the public relations plan or counter adverse publicity.

Concept note-3: -Outdoor advertising, also known as out-of-home advertising, is advertising that reaches the consumer while they are outside of their home. Examples of outdoor advertising include billboards, bus shelters, kiosks, and transit advertising.
