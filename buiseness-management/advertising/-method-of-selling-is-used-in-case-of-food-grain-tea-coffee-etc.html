____ method of selling is used in case of food-grain, tea, coffee etc.

(A) Sale by inspection

(B) Sale by Description

(C) Sale on Approval

(D) ** Sale by sample

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The best method of agricultural marketing is the open auction method. Here, the prospective buyers gather at the shop of a commission agent around the heap of the produce, examine it and offer bids loudly. The produce is given to the highest bidder.

Concept note-2: -Agricultural marketing is mainly the buying and selling of agricultural products.
