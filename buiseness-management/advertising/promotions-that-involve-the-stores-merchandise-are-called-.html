Promotions that involve the store’s merchandise are called ____

(A) ** Merchandise Promotions

(B) Institutional PromotionInstuituitional Promotions

(C) Sales Promotions

(D) Publicity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Merchandising is the practice of promoting a product or brand through the use of advertising, promotions, and other marketing techniques. It is a key component of any retail business and is used to increase sales and create brand recognition.

Concept note-2: -Vouchers and coupons It’s easy, the more people buy out of your store, the more coupons they get. Let’s say that for 10 coupons, they get a 50% discount for a certain product line from your store.

Concept note-3: -Promotional merchandise are products branded with a logo or slogan and distributed at little or no cost to promote a brand, corporate identity, or event. Such products, which are often informally called promo products, swag (mass nouns), tchotchkes, or freebies (count nouns), are used in marketing and sales.

Concept note-4: -Retail merchandising is the practice of displaying products in a way that encourages shoppers to purchase them. This includes the placement of products in stores, the selection of items to be placed on shelves and the design of store displays.
