Online advertising is a form of ____

(A) ** Advertising

(B) Designing

(C) Promotion

(D) Marketing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Online advertising, also known as online marketing, Internet advertising, digital advertising or web advertising, is a form of marketing and advertising which uses the Internet to promote products and services to audiences and platform users.

Concept note-2: -The 8 main types of online ads include product listing ads, display ads, demand-side platform ads, affiliate ads, native ads, social media ads, video ads, and email ads.

Concept note-3: -Advertising gets to people through different forms of communication. Newspapers, magazines and direct mail belong to print media. TV, radio and the Internet are among the most important electronic media.

Concept note-4: -Paid search – also known as pay-per-click advertising, or PPC – is one of the most common and effective types of online advertising. Paid search allows you to bid on relevant terms and phrases that may cause text-based ads to be displayed to users when they enter specific search queries into Google or Bing.
