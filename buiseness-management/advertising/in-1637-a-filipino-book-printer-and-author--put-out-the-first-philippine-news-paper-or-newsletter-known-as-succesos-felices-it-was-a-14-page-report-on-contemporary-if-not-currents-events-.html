In 1637 a Filipino book printer and author, ____ put out the first Philippine news paper or newsletter known as Succesos Felices-it was a 14 page report on contemporary if not currents events .

(A) Johann Gutenberg

(B) William Caxton

(C) ** Tomas Pinpin

(D) Benjamin Harris

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1637 he published and printed what is thought to be the first newspaper in the Philippines, the 14-page “Sucesos Felices", that reported mainly on Spanish military victories. It is estimated that all throughout his career, from 1609 to 1639, Tomas Pinpin printed at least fourteen different publications.

Concept note-2: -1637-Successos Felices (Fortunate Events) is the first Philippine newsletter was launched by Tomas Pinpin who is known as “The Father of Filipino Printing".

Concept note-3: -The first newspaper was established in the Philippines in 1811. The paper, Del Superior Govierno, was published with the Spanish Gover-nor General himself as editor.

Concept note-4: -Doctrina Christiana-The first book printed in the Philippines, Manila, 1593.

Concept note-5: -Doctrina Christiana: The First Book Printed in the Philippines.
