Concept note-1: -The definition of advertising is an industry used to call the attention of the public to something, typically a product or service. Concept note-2: -Definition: Advertising is a means of communication with the users of a product or service. Advertisements are messages paid for by those who send them and are intended to inform or influence people who receive them, as defined by the Advertising Association of the UK.
