ADVERTISING

[SOURCES]
Retentive advertising is done to improve social welfare of the community

(A) true

(B) ** false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -advertising aimed at reminding a target market that a product is available as opposed to informing or persuading it; typically associated with products in the mature stage of their life cycle. Also referred to as Retentive Advertising.

Concept note-2: -TRUE. Advertising promotes ideas, goods and services.

Concept note-3: -These kinds of advertisements are called social advertisements.

Concept note-4: -Public service announcements (PSAs) are a category of institutional advertising focused on social-welfare issues such as drunk driving, drug use, and practicing a healthy lifestyle. Usually PSAs are sponsored by nonprofit organizations and government agencies with a vested interest in the causes they promote.
