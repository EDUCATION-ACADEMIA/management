EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An advertising agency, often referred to as a creative agency, is a business dedicated to creating, planning, and handling advertising and sometimes other forms of promotion and marketing for its clients.

Concept note-2: -Full-service agency provides all advertising services to the client. Generally, ad agencies get commission from media of 15 percent.

Concept note-3: -A creative boutique agency is a small agency that focuses on customized services. These agencies focus on making an extremely creative and innovative form of branding and advertising. They generally work for small, mid-market, and B2B companies.

Concept note-4: -What Is a Creative Agency? A creative agency is a term for an agency that offers a variety of services that fall under the umbrella of marketing and advertising. Basically, if you need any type of creative strategy, work, or promotion, they can help you get it done.
