What is the correct order when creating an ad campaign?

(A) ** identify target market, determine objectives, establish budget, develop message, select media, evaluate campaign

(B) evaluate campaign, determine objectives, identify target market, develop message, select media, establish budget

(C) establish budget, identify target market, evaluate campaign, determine objectives, develop message, select media

(D) determine objectives, select media, identify target market, establish budget, develop message, evaluate campaign

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Step 1: Determine Your Objective and Budget. Step 2: Identify Your Target Audience. Step 3: Create Your Message. Step 4: Develop Your Media Strategy.

Concept note-2: -Objectives of advertising can be used as a way of stimulating sales, attract customers and create brand and product loyalty, create a confident and enthusiastic customer base, and achieving a competitive advantage over the market competition.
