The audience figure is ____ ____ than its circulation because more than one person can read a single issue of a printed newspaper or magazine.

(A) normally lower

(B) ** normally higher

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Readership figures are usually higher than circulation figures because of the assumption that a typical copy is read by more than one person.

Concept note-2: -Circulation is a count of how many copies of a particular publication are distributed.

Concept note-3: -Rise in literacy rate: due to the steady rise in literacy rates in all the states of our country, more and more people are being initiated into the world of reading books, newspapers, and periodicals.

Concept note-4: -Traditionally, newspaper audiences have been more educated, affluent and older than non-newspaper readers.
