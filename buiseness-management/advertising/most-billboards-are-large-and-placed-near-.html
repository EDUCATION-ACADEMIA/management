Most billboards are large and placed near ____

(A) airports

(B) malls

(C) ** highways

(D) grocery stores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Billboards are typically placed in high traffic areas, such as along highways and in cities, so they’re seen by the highest number of drivers and pedestrians. Billboard advertising is effective for building brand awareness and broadcasting your business (or product or campaign) to as many people as possible.

Concept note-2: -Examples of outdoor advertising include billboards, bus shelters, kiosks, and transit advertising.

Concept note-3: -Transit advertising usually consists of posters placed on the sides of buses, in subway stations, inside trains, and at airports. Broadcast media are the most effective means of advertising.

Concept note-4: -One advantage of outdoor ads is that they offer timely exposure due to their proximity to shopping areas and vehicles on the way to shopping areas.

Concept note-5: -Prime time or the peak time is the block of broadcast programming taking place during the middle of the evening for a television show. It is mostly targeted towards adults (and sometimes families). It is used by the major television networks to broadcast their season’s nightly programming.
