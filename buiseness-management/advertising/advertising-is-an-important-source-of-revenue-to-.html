Advertising is an important source of revenue to ____

(A) Advertisers

(B) Public

(C) ** Media

(D) Government

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In many countries advertising is the most important source of income for the media (e.g., newspapers, magazines, or television stations) through which it is conducted. In the noncommunist world advertising has become a large and important service industry.

Concept note-2: -The major source of revenue for mass media is advertising.

Concept note-3: -Advertising is a powerful tool that can help you attract customers, inform them about your products and build customer trust. Most companies use some type of advertisement to help them promote their products or services.

Concept note-4: -Solution: Advertisements are the main source of income for media.

Concept note-5: -How does ad revenue work? You can earn ad revenue by owning content that receives enough attention for companies to purchase space on. When the visitors to your webpage or viewers of your content click on or experience the advertisement, you often receive payment for that.
