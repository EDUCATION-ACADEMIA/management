A ____ is a channel or system of communication.

(A) Infomercial

(B) Web-cast

(C) ** Medium

(D) Pop-up ad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A medium in communication is a system or channel through which a speaker or writer addresses their audience. Itâ€™s an outlet that a sender uses to express meaning to their audience, and it can include written, verbal or nonverbal elements. A communication medium can either be virtual or physical.

Concept note-2: -A communication channel is the medium, mean, manner or method through which a message is sent to its intended receiver. The basic channels are written (hard copy print or digital formats), oral or spoken, and electronic and multimedia.

Concept note-3: -A communication medium is a channel or route in the communication process through which a sender communicates their message to the receiver. This can take various forms which will be elaborated on later in this reading but a few examples could include emails, voice messages, etc.

Concept note-4: -4 Types of Communication: Verbal, Non-verbal, Written, Visual.

Concept note-5: -Communication channels can be categorized into three principal channels: (1) verbal, (2) written, and (3) non-verbal. Each of these communications channels have different strengths and weaknesses, and oftentimes we can use more than one channel at the same time.
