ADVERTISING

[SOURCES]
____ advertising is aimed at creating awareness about any social cause.

(A) ** social

(B) social media

(C) brand

(D) mass media

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Social advertising is the use of advertising to inform the public about a social issue or to influence their behavior.

Concept note-2: -The Purpose of Advertising Advertising has three primary objectives: to inform, to persuade, and to remind. Informative Advertising creates awareness of brands, products, services, and ideas. It announces new products and programs and can educate people about the attributes and benefits of new or established products.

Concept note-3: -While small businesses primarily use advertising to deliver information on their products and services, advertising methods also raise awareness of social issues. Powerful images, resonant music and well-worded text can evoke feelings in viewers to promote societal changes.

Concept note-4: -Advertising helps to make consumers aware of a product and aims to build preference for that product over its competitors. If advertising succeeds in those two tasks, consumers will choose the advertised product when they make their next purchase.
