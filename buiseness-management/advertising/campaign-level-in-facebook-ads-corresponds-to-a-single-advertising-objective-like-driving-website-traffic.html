Campaign level in Facebook ads corresponds to a single advertising objective, like driving website traffic.

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A campaign is a series of ad sets and ads that aim to accomplish a single objective, like generating leads or increasing the number of app installs. Campaigns contain one or more ad sets. Ad sets contain one or more ads. At the campaign level, youâ€™ll choose an ad objective to define your goals.

Concept note-2: -Each Facebook ad campaign is made up of three parts. At the campaign level, you choose your objective, which is the action you want people to take when they see your ads. Facebook has three objective categories: Awareness, Consideration, and Conversion. Choose from 11 Facebook advertising objectives.

Concept note-3: -Facebook organizes ads in a structure with three levels: campaign, ad set and ad. In the API, developers have access to a fourth level called creative.
