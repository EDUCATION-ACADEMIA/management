ADVERTISING

[SOURCES]
Billboards provide the most common form of outdoor advertising.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why Billboards? Billboards and out of home advertising are the most effective advertising medium for each stage of the customer journey. Out of home advertising is perfect for reaching a larger audience on a daily basis. An incredible 86% of people say that outdoor advertising really makes brands stand out for them.

Concept note-2: -Billboards are the most common form of outdoor advertising. They come in various sizes. They range from static to digital, or mobile. Thanks to new technology, some of the newer ones use augmented reality.

Concept note-3: -Outdoor advertising, also known as out-of-home advertising, is advertising that reaches the consumer while they are outside of their home. Examples of outdoor advertising include billboards, bus shelters, kiosks, and transit advertising.

Concept note-4: -The possibilities for outdoor advertising are endless, but there are two key categories in which they can be placed: static and digital. Traditional, or “classic” outdoor media refers to placements that are static. This includes Billboards or Bulletins, Wallscapes, Posters, Transit Ads, and more.

Concept note-5: -The most popular and effective form of outdoor advertising is billboard advertising, which promotes a product, service, or event using large, printed signs. It is common for billboards to be placed along busy roads and highways where motorists and passengers can easily see them.
