A slogan is

(A) ** short memorable phrases to identify a product

(B) catchy image

(C) A large billboard on side of the road

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A slogan is a memorable phrase or motto intended to be a repetitive expression of a purpose or promise. A slogan aims to subliminally persuade potential customers into buying a product or service by describing the company’s offerings in a quick and fun way.

Concept note-2: -A catchy and easy to remember slogan is one that will make the reader remember which company it was associated with; hence, the purpose of the slogan. A slogan that is catchy and describes the personality of your service or business is optimum.

Concept note-3: -Similar in form to a tagline, typically a few words or a short sentence, a slogan is most often used to represent a specific product line or individual marketing campaign.

Concept note-4: -A slogan is a memorable phrase that’s often used in commercial advertising in a repetitive manner. These slogans are sometimes associated with a particular product, although it’s also common for brands to create more generalized slogans.

Concept note-5: -A tagline is a phrase that highlights what a business does, emphasizes a value or specific message, and/or clarifies a brand’s mission. You can think of taglines as a brand’s mission statement condensed into a few words.
