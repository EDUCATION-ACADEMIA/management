Stating that this product offer expires in three days so buy now is an example of:

(A) Sales and price

(B) Games and activities

(C) ** Urgency

(D) Special ingredients

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is urgency in sales? Urgency in sales is a technique that professionals use to create the sense that their audience and prospective customers must act immediately if they wish to purchase your product or receive it for the best price. Creating urgency helps motivate customers to buy now rather than later.

Concept note-2: -When you set a deadline, you’re telling your prospect that they need to take action now or they’ll miss out. For example, you could say, “Our offer expires at the end of the day, ” or “You need to buy today to get the special price.” These types of statements will create sales urgency and motivate your prospects to buy.
