Concept note-1: -Headline. If you want to generate a measurable response to your Web page or ad, you MUST have a headline. An ad with just photos, words, videos, or illustrations may impress people and it may get their attention, but it will not generate actual measurable response without a headline.

Concept note-2: -Word-of-mouth marketing is one of the best ways to attract customer attention and ultimately new customers. Statistics say that 90% of consumers value the recommendations they get from their families, friends, and colleagues, while 74% find word-of-mouth a key influencing factor when making their purchasing decisions.
