This participant in the promotional programme has the products to be marketed and assumes major responsibility for developing the marketing programme and making final decisions regarding the marketing communications programme

(A) ** Advertiser

(B) Public Relations firm

(C) Media organisation

(D) Advertising agency

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Your marketing department should create the materials that describe and promote your core products and/or services. They should be kept up-to-date as those products and services evolve.

Concept note-2: -Advertisers are possibly the most important participant in the IMC process. They are the ones who have the product or services to be marketed and the ones who provide the funds for the projects to be created.

Concept note-3: -Direct marketing is a promotional method that involves presenting information about your company, product, or service to your target customer without the use of an advertising middleman.

Concept note-4: -The six major modes of communication in marketing include advertising, digital marketing, direct marketing, personal selling, public relations and sales promotion.
