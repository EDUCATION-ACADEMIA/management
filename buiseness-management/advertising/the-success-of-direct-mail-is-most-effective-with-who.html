The success of direct mail is most effective with who?

(A) new customers

(B) ** existing customers

(C) elderly customers

(D) no one-no one likes direct mail

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Direct mail has proven its value time and time again. Statistics and science prove it often yields greater results than digital because of its tangibility. Direct mail also offers major benefits, such as greater response rates, trust building, personalization, versatility, simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and more.

Concept note-2: -Direct mail boasts better response rates, greater visibility, and more creative opportunities than online efforts, so it should be a key component of any marketing campaign.

Concept note-3: -Because it communicates directly with customers, it can deliver a higher conversion rate (the percentage of the targeted group that, for example, buys your product) at a lower cost.
