What is an advertising jingle?

(A) The bells on a sleigh.

(B) ** A song that helps sell a product or service.

(C) My dog’s name.

(D) The main idea of a commercial.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A jingle is a short song or tune used in advertising and for other commercial uses. Jingles are a form of sound branding. A jingle contains one or more hooks and meaning that explicitly promote the product or service being advertised, usually through the use of one or more advertising slogans.

Concept note-2: -A jingle is a short, simple tune, often with words, which is used to advertise a product or programme on radio or television.

Concept note-3: -Lucky Charms “They’re Magically Delicious” Huggies “I’m a Big Kid Now” Alka Seltzer “Plop, Fizz” Band-Aid “Stuck on Band-Aid"

Concept note-4: -: to rhyme or sound in a catchy repetitious manner. transitive verb. : to cause to jingle.

Concept note-5: -Jingles Attract Customers In addition to enhancing brand awareness, creating a message that will stick in a listener’s memory, jingles serve one other crucial purpose: attracting customers.
