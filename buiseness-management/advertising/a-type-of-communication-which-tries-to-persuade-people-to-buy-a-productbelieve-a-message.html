ADVERTISING

[SOURCES]
A type of communication which tries to persuade people to buy a product/believe a message.

(A) spokesperson

(B) testimonial

(C) endorsement

(D) ** advertisement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Persuasive advertising is an advertising technique that can help you attract the interest of your key target audiences and convince them to give your product a try without being overly educational and informative.

Concept note-2: -Persuasive communications contain a variety of attributes intended to enhance persuasion, which could include an attractive source, a message containing convincing arguments, or efforts to make the topic seem personally relevant to the audience.

Concept note-3: -Advertising is persuasive communication sent to consumers by companies attempting to sell products or services.

Concept note-4: -The persuasive strategies used by advertisers who want you to buy their product can be divided into three categories: pathos, logos, and ethos. Pathos: an appeal to emotion. An advertisement using pathos will attempt to evoke an emotional response in the consumer.
