Which of the following situations is an example of a business using telemarketing for promotional purposes:

(A) A community bank gives a customer a personal CD player when s/he opens a checking account.

(B) A local car dealer places a full-size advertisement in several community papers.

(C) An interactive kiosk allows customers to design and personalize their greeting cards.

(D) ** A digital communications company calls customers to announce new options in wireless service.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Telemarketing is the practice of marketing products or services over the phone. An example of telemarketing is a company calling potential customers to offer them a discount if they sign up for their services.

Concept note-2: -Digital marketing and telemarketing are two different marketing strategies. When used properly, however, they undoubtedly complement each other. Digital marketing is known to allow marketers to reach a large number of people with minimal resources. Telemarketing, on the other hand, makes a personal connection.

Concept note-3: -Telemarketing is when a business sells products or services over the phone. It is also known as telesales. Telemarketing can be used to complete the sales process or to arrange further face-to-face or online sales appointments. It is a type of direct marketing.
