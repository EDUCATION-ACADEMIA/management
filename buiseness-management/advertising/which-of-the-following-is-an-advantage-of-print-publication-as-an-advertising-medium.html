Which of the following is an advantage of print publication as an advertising medium?

(A) Is very expensive

(B) Large volumes of viewers

(C) ** Easy to focus on a target audience

(D) Disappears from view in a matter of seconds

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Magazine audiences are highly targeted, allowing marketers to easily target relevant consumers without wasting budget. Audiences are generally more interested in your advertising and expect it.

Concept note-2: -In addition to the sensory aspects, print media is widely considered more credible than digital media. People who subscribe to magazines and newspapers do so for a reason-they believe the information contained in the publication is accurate, credible and provides them with some benefit.

Concept note-3: -Pros of Print Media: Still gives a higher ROI than digital ads. Enable more visibility to small businesses. Produce a loyal customer base. Reach multiple generations.

Concept note-4: -Newspaper advertising typically costs less per thousand readers than television, radio, and direct mail advertising. In addition, newspaper staff members will work directly with advertisers to create ads at no additional costs. Newspaper advertising can also be customized to meet any budget.
