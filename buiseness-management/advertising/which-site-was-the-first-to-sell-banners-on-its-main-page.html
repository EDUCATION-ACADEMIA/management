Which site was the first to sell banners on its main page?

(A) ** Yahoo!

(B) Altavista

(C) Google

(D) MSN

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The banner ad that’s widely described as the first ever was a little rectangle purchased by AT&T on HotWired.com in 1994. About 44 percent of the people who saw it actually clicked on it.

Concept note-2: -In October 1994 the first online banner ad was born. It simply read: “Have you ever clicked your mouse right HERE? YOU WILL” and was published on HotWired.com – now better known as Wired.com – for a campaign by AT&T.

Concept note-3: -On May 8, 1704, one of the first newspaper ads appeared in the Boston News-Letter. It was a real estate advertisement selling a plantation on Oyster Bay, Long island.

Concept note-4: -1. Banner advertising, also called display advertising, consists of static or animated images or media and is usually placed in high-visibility areas on high-traffic websites.
