The cost to air an ad during a particular television show depends on the of the ____ show.

(A) cast

(B) content

(C) ** popularity

(D) producers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is The popularity of the channel. The cost to advertise on a news channel mainly depends on the popularity of the channel.

Concept note-2: -Expensive: Advertising in magazines can be more expensive than advertising in newspapers. Competition: Many magazines have similar audiences, which leads to increased competition for reader attention and ad space.

Concept note-3: -Media planning is the process by which marketers determine how, when, and where an audience is given a selected advertising message. Media planners analyze the audiences, channels, and advertisements to determine the most efficient way to communicate a message to the intended audience.

Concept note-4: -Prime time or the peak time is the block of broadcast programming taking place during the middle of the evening for a television show.
