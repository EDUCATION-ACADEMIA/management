Any Form Of Impersonal (One-Way) Paid Communication In Which The Company Is Identified

(A) Advergaming

(B) Advertainment

(C) Personal Selling

(D) ** Advertising

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Promotion is defined as any form of impersonal, paid communication in which the sponsor or company is identified.

Concept note-2: -Advertising is a form of impersonal, one-way mass communication that is paid for by the sponsor.

Concept note-3: -Impersonal advertising, on the other hand, requires labor to create the content, but such delivery channels as newspaper ads, television commercials and Internet banners go to many people simultaneously, without the need for personal interaction.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Advertising is defined as the impersonal form of communication which is paid form by the marketer to promote some goods or services. It is commonly use as the promotional tool of the company.

Concept note-5: -Advertising is any paid form of communication from an identified sponsor or source that draws attention to ideas, goods, services or the sponsor itself-essentially commercials and ads (whether digital or print).
