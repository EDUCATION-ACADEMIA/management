ADVERTISING

[SOURCES]
Print ads are not complete without the name of the advertiser, or the sponsor. The ____ (name of the advertiser) or logotype (logo) is the distinctive identification symbol for a business.

(A) ** signature

(B) headline

(C) illustration

(D) copy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The signature *, or logotype (logo), is the distinctive identification symbol for a business.

Concept note-2: -What are the four elements of a print ad? They are Headline, Illustration, Body Copy and Signature. You need to make sure every print ad has these parts. The most important is the illustration (image).

Concept note-3: -Print advertising means advertisements that are printed in hard copy in publications (newspapers, magazines, journals) likely to be read by your target audience.

Concept note-4: -A brand is a unique identifying symbol, logo, mark, personality, character, name, word, and sentence that business use to distinguish their products from others. A combination of one or more of those components can be utilized to create a Brand Label. Legal protection given to a brand is called a trademark.
