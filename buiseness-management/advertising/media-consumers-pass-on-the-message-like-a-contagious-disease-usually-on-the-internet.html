ADVERTISING

[SOURCES]
Media consumers pass on the message, like a contagious disease, usually on the internet

(A) Live advertisement

(B) Poster campaign

(C) ** Viral advertising

(D) Word-of-mouth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The known effects of advertising on consumers range from creating an awareness of the product or service to influencing a buying decision. Advertising can create a shift in thinking by consumers, which may take different forms.

Concept note-2: -Viral marketing is a style of promotion that relies on an audience to organically generate and push the message of a product or service. On social media, marketing is considered “viral” when it’s being shared rapidly by the public at large (with a compounding effect) rather than just its target audience.

Concept note-3: -Companies use many different forms of media to communicate sales promotions, such as printed materials like posters, coupons, direct mail pieces and billboards, radio and television ads, digital media (like text messages), email, websites, social media, and so forth.
