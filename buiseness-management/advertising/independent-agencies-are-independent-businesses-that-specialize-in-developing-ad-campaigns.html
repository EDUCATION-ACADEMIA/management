Independent agencies are independent businesses that specialize in developing ad campaigns

(A) Advertising Campaign

(B) ** Advertising Agency

(C) Freelancer

(D) Media

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An ad agency is generally independent of the client; it may be an internal department or agency that provides an outside point of view to the effort of selling the client’s products or services, or an outside firm.

Concept note-2: -An advertising agency, often referred to as a creative agency, is a company providing services associated with creating, planning, and managing advertising campaigns, but may also handle other forms of promotion and marketing for its clients.

Concept note-3: -Creative agency definition. What is an advertising agency? Advertising agencies sometimes referred to as creative agencies, are responsible for creating and managing their clients’ advertising campaigns. Ad agencies work with marketing teams to develop a strategy to help clients meet their goals.

Concept note-4: -A primary role of an advertising agency is the creation of an advertising and marketing plan specific to your business, product and brand. Ad agencies work with your business objectives, keep within ad budgets and develop advertising and marketing campaigns to satisfy a business’ needs.
