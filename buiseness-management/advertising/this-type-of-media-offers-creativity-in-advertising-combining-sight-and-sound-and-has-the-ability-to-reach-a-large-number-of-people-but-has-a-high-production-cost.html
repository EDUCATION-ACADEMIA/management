ADVERTISING

[SOURCES]
This type of media offers creativity in advertising, combining sight and sound and has the ability to reach a large number of people, but has a high production cost.

(A) ** Television

(B) Radio

(C) Newspaper

(D) Specialty

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Television is considered as the most ideal form of advertising medium and has exhibited the most rapid growth among other advertising mediums. Its ability to combine visual images, sound, motion, and colour presents the advertiser with the opportunity to develop the most creative and imaginative appeals of any medium.

Concept note-2: -There are four major types of advertisement mediums: print, television, outdoor signage, and digital media advertisements.

Concept note-3: -Out-of-home advertising allows marketers to focus on a particular geographic area and is relatively inexpensive. Messages, though, must be limited to a few words because the audience is usually moving.

Concept note-4: -Broadcast Advertising-This type of advertising is very popular all around the world. It consists of television, radio, or Internet advertising.
