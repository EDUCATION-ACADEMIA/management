This media includes television and radio, meaning that a signal is transmitted.

(A) Print

(B) ** Broadcast

(C) Outdoor

(D) Direct Mail

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Broadcast media describes the traditional forms of media that include television and radio. Technically, the term ‘broadcast media’ can include the internet as well and even such things as Bluetooth marketing and other forms of location-based transmissions.

Concept note-2: -Broadcast Signal means a television or radio signal that is transmitted over-the-air to a wide geographic audience and is received by the cable communications system off-the-air, whether by microwave link, by satellite receiver, or by other means.

Concept note-3: -The term ‘broadcast media’ covers a wide spectrum of different communication methods such as television, radio, newspapers, magazines and any other materials supplied by the media and press.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Simplex Communication.

Concept note-5: -Various frequencies of radio waves are used for television and FM and AM radio broadcasts, military communications, mobile phones, ham radio, wireless computer networks, and numerous other communications applications.
