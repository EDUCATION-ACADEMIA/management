The ____ marked a great expansion in advertisement in US as business prospered during the Industrial revolution and the print media recorded spectacular growth

(A) ** 19th century

(B) 18th century

(C) 20th century

(D) none of the options

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Newspapers, which had become cheaper and more widely available, were the perfect way to reach this mass market of potential customers. These first advertisements just had simple descriptions of the products, with the price. By the mid-19th century it was possible to add illustrations.

Concept note-2: -One of the first English printed advertisements was a handbill created in 1477 by an English merchant, diplomat, and writer, named William Caxton. This small broadside was intended to be displayed in the neighborhood outside his shop in Westminster Abbey.

Concept note-3: -In the ancient and medieval world such advertising as existed was conducted by word of mouth. The first step toward modern advertising came with the development of printing in the 15th and 16th centuries.

Concept note-4: -1. Pre-printing period: In this period (prior to the fifteenth century) the town criers were means of advertising.
