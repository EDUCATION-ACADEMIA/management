What is Facebook Ads Manager?

(A) It is where you can monitor ads campaigns across multiple advertising platforms owned by Facebook, including Instagram ads.

(B) It is an ad management tool to make, edit and analyze paid promotional Facebook campaigns.

(C) ** all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ads Manager is your starting point for running ads on Facebook, Instagram, Messenger or Audience Network. It’s an all-in-one tool for creating ads, managing when and where they’ll run, and tracking how well your campaigns are performing towards your marketing goals.

Concept note-2: -To navigate to Ads Manager right from your business Page, head to the left sidebar and click on the “Ad Center” dropdown arrow of any Facebook Page, then choose “All Ads” from the dropdown. At the bottom of that Page, there is an option to click “Ads Manager."
