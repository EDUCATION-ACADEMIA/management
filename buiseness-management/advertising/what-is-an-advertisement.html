What is an advertisement?

(A) A tv commercial

(B) A short video trying to sell something

(C) A billboard

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An advertisement, otherwise known as an advert or ad, is generally considered a public communication that promotes a product, service, brand or event. To some the definition can be even broader than that, extending to any paid communication designed to inform or influence.

Concept note-2: -Advertising is a marketing tactic involving paying for space to promote a product, service, or cause. The actual promotional messages are called advertisements, or ads for short. The goal of advertising is to reach people most likely to be willing to pay for a companyâ€™s products or services and entice them to buy.

Concept note-3: -The purpose of the advertisement is to persuade and enlighten consumers about any service or good available on the market.

Concept note-4: -A full position ad refers to an advertisement that either has content matter on both sides of it, or that is located on the top portion of a column and contains content matter on one side. It is often used in print media, such as newspapers and magazines, but it can also be used in Internet marketing.
