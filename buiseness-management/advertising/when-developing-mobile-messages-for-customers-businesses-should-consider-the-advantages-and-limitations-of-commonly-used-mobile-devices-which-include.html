When developing mobile messages for customers, businesses should consider the advantages and limitations of commonly used mobile devices, which include:

(A) smartphones, servers, and tablets

(B) dashboards, smartphones, and electronic readers

(C) servers, smartphones, and dashboards

(D) ** electronic readers, tablets, and smartphones

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Advantages of mobile devices in business The advantages of mobile devices make it possible for users to work wherever and whenever they need to, while still providing them access to corporate resources. They can take their offices with them, whether traveling, commuting, working in the field or desk-bound at home.

Concept note-2: -Mobile computing involves mobile communication, mobile hardware, and mobile software. Communication issues include ad hoc networks and infrastructure networks as well as communication properties, protocols, data formats, and concrete technologies. Hardware includes mobile devices or device components.
