News release, featured article, captioned photograph ad press conference are the example of which public relations tool?

(A) Written and spoken communication

(B) Event sponsorship

(C) ** Publicity

(D) Corporate identity materials

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Public relations can be: proactive – for example, sharing positive news stories to raise your business profile. reactive – for example, responding to an event that can have a negative impact on your business’s reputation.

Concept note-2: -Advertising is media you pay for, public relations is media you earn through the building of a brand or product or the like. Enhancing public relations involves building trust and credibility, whereas advertising includes building basic exposure and company overhead.
