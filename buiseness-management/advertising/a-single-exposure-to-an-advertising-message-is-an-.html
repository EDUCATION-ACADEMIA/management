A single exposure to an advertising message is an ____

(A) media ad

(B) episode

(C) ** impression

(D) option

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A single exposure to an advertising message is called an impression *. The number of people exposed to an ad. A single exposure to an advertising message. Frequency * is the number of times an audience sees or hears an advertisement.

Concept note-2: -impression. a single exposure to an advertising message. frequency. number of times an audience sees or hears an ad.

Concept note-3: -Single image ads advertising specifications Single image ads are a Sponsored Content ad format. The ads include one image and appear directly in your campaign’s target audience member’s feed.

Concept note-4: -Examples of advertisements include print ads, radio and television commercials, infomercials, advertorials, and billboards and kiosks–both of which may be stagnant or interactive.

Concept note-5: -In the context of advertising, frequency can be defined as the number of times a person is exposed to an advertisement or the number of times an advertisement is repeated through a specific medium over a specific time period.
