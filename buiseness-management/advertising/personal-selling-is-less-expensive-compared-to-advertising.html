Personal Selling is less expensive compared to advertising

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cost: Advertising can be expensive, especially for TV and print ads, whereas personal selling is generally less expensive as it involves direct communication with potential customers.

Concept note-2: -Personal selling is more expensive than advertising.

Concept note-3: -Because personal selling occurs face-to-face, salespeople are required. Therefore, it is more expensive because it requires training, hiring, and payment of sales people.

Concept note-4: -It is a relatively expensive method of selling. There is a requirement of high capital costs. Also, it is an extremely labour intensive method because a large sales force is required to carry out personal selling successfully. The training of the salesperson is also a very time consuming and costly process.
