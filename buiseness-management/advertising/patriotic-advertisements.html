Concept note-1: -The phrase patriotic marketing is that form of marketing when the theme of the marketing campaign aligns itself with the feeling of patriotism. There are slogans, images and catchphrases to lure people and convey a message of pride and honour for the country.

Concept note-2: -Patriotic appeal is the love feeling towards the country which is used in the advertising to make it more effective. It is used by different companies in their advertising campaigns to break the clutter and to position their brands in the mind of customer.

Concept note-3: -Brand advertising is a form of advertising which helps establish connections and build strong, long-term relationships with consumers over time. Companies that use brand advertising aim to get long-term positive recognition.
