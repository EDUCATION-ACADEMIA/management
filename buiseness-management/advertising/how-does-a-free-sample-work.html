How does a free sample work?

(A) It encourages people to buy again at a later date.

(B) It enters customers into a prize draw.

(C) It rewards regular customers for their loyalty.

(D) ** Customers get to try something before buying it.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Giving out free samples to the target market is a way to quickly gain many customers because you effectively get your product or service into the hands of the people who are most likely to want what you are selling. In addition, people will try something new if it doesn’t cost them any money.

Concept note-2: -Product sampling is when companies provide consumers and potential customers in their target audience with a free sample of their product. It’s a great way to let customers “try before they buy, ” building trust and increasing the likelihood that they’ll choose to make a full purchase in the future.

Concept note-3: -Here are a few ways free samples will benefit both you and your customers: Introduce your product to new audiences unfamiliar with your business. Foster relationships and inspire loyalty with existing customers. Expand guests’ knowledge about the products you offer.
