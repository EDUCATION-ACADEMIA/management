What question do you ask to get feedback to see if the computer software met expectations of the customer?

(A) Did they deliver it on time?

(B) . Did you have installation problems?

(C) What new features have you used?

(D) ** How has the software worked for what you were using it for?

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Terms in this set (12) what question do you ask to get feedback to see if the computer software met expectations of the customer? answer is D how has the software worked for what you were using if for.

Concept note-2: -Collecting Customer Feedback Helps to Increase Customer Loyalty, if Used Well. Customer experiences drive buying decisions and are directly related to brand loyalty.
