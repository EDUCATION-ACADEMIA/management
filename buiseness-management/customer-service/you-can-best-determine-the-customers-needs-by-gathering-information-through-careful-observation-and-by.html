CUSTOMER SERVICE

[SOURCES]
You can best determine the customer’s needs by gathering information through careful observation and by:

(A) Deciding the type of products you think the customer should buy

(B) Telling the customer everything you know about your products

(C) ** Asking the customer thoughtful questions

(D) Studying all literature on the products you sell so you can answer questions

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can best determine the customer’s needs by gathering information through careful observation and by: Asking the customer thoughtful questions. What is an example of an open ended question? What features are important to you?

Concept note-2: -Show respectfully that you understand why they are upset, and try to put yourself in their position to see how you might feel in a similar situation. This might help place you in a more understanding frame of mind to craft a solution. Verbalize your sympathy and apologize clearly and unequivocally.

Concept note-3: -"Customer service means listening to customers and helping them resolve their issues quickly.

Concept note-4: -How would you handle a situation where a customer wants a brand that you don’t carry? Get permission from him to show the items you do have that meets his needs.
