EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Early adopters are those individuals that use new products before the majority of people.

Concept note-2: -Early adopters will be targeted following innovators, they too may be approached prior to a product launch and again emphasis will be placed on research into what this sector needs.

Concept note-3: -Some buyers and sellers are more interested in building strong relationships with one another than others. The four types of relationships between buyers and sellers are transactional, functional, affiliative, and strategic.
