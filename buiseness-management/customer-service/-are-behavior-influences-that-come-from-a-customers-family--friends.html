CUSTOMER SERVICE

[SOURCES]
____ are behavior influences that come from a customer’s family & friends.

(A) External commercial cues

(B) ** External social cues

(C) Internal physical cues

(D) Retailer cues

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Forces that other people exert on buying behavior are called social influences. They are divided into five major groups: roles, family, reference groups and opinion leaders, social classes, and culture and subcultures.

Concept note-2: -In general, there are four factors that influence consumer behaviour. These factors impact whether or not your target customer buys your product. They are cultural, social, personal and psychological.

Concept note-3: -Psychological, Cultural, Social and Personal are the four factors that affect consumer behaviour.

Concept note-4: -Consumer behavior is the study of customers’ buying choices and the motivation behind those decisions. Marketers can determine consumers’ preferences and buying patterns by examining consumer buying behavior. Cultural, social, personal, and psychological factors could influence consumer buying behavior.
