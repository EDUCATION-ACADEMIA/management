Brand loyal customers:

(A) Always purchase from the same store

(B) ** Ask for a particular make by name

(C) Either of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Brand loyalty is when customers continue to purchase from the same brand over and over again, despite competitors offering similar products or services. Not only do customers continue engaging and purchasing from the same brand, but they also associate positive feelings toward that brand.

Concept note-2: -Brand loyalty is when a customer has had positive experiences with a product or service and therefore makes repeat purchases over time. High-quality products, exceptional customer experience, satisfying customer service, and shared values are all factors that influence brand loyalty.

Concept note-3: -Brand loyalty-repeat purchases of a particular brand based on the perception of higher quality and better service than any competitor-is not dependent on price.

Concept note-4: -Hard-core Loyals: Customers who buy exclusively from a brand. Split Loyals: Customers loyal to two or three brands in a particular category. Shifting Loyals: Customers who move from one brand to another.
