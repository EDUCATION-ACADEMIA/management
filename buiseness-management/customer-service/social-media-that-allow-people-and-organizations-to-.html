Social media that allow people and organizations to ____

(A) Get answers to questions

(B) Call your family

(C) ** Post information about themselves, product and services

(D) Show your happiness

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marketing professionals do this by creating social media posts, answering questions in the comment section, hosting virtual product launches, posting stories or promoting fun contests or giveaways that involve company products.

Concept note-2: -Shared blogging platforms like Medium and Tumblr give people a space to express their thoughts and help connect them with readers.

Concept note-3: -Social news A social news site allows its users to post news links and other items to external articles. Users then proceed to vote on said items, and the items with the highest number of votes are most prominently displayed. A good example of a social news site is Reddit.

Concept note-4: -Blogging networks allow you to publish content for your audience and help them discover it. This type of social media is a great way to build engagement and get people familiar with your business.
