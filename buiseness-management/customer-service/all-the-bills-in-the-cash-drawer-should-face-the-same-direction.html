All the bills in the cash drawer should face the same direction?

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The currency should be separated into slots by denomination, portrait side up, with all facing in the same direction. The highest denomination should always be on the left, decreasing to the smallest on the far right.

Concept note-2: -For a small business, $100 to $150 should be more than enough. A good rule of thumb is to keep at least $20 in five-dollar bills and $20 in one-dollar bills.

Concept note-3: -Individuals should keep working cash funds to a minimum at all times. Excess funds should be in a locked device or deposited in the Cashierâ€™s Office. Cash received must not be used for making change, petty cash purposes, etc. Expenditures or refunds cannot be made from cash receipts.
