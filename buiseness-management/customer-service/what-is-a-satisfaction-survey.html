What is a satisfaction survey?

(A) An instrument which gives a business no guidance of how well they are doing

(B) It is used instead of training employees

(C) Used to inform customers of new product ranges

(D) ** An instrument that helps businesses measure their customers’ level of satisfaction with their product/service

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It’s a tool to measure how a customer feels at the moment. The customer satisfaction score is the most common method used to gauge consumer sentiment. You can calculate CSAT using a scale of 1-3 to 1-10 and anything in between.

Concept note-2: -1. Net Promoter Score. Net Promoter Score ®, or NPS, is a popular customer satisfaction survey used to gather quantitative and qualitative customer data.

Concept note-3: -A customer satisfaction survey, or a CSAT survey, is a questionnaire used by companies to understand how satisfied customers are with its branding, products, services, or customer support experience.
