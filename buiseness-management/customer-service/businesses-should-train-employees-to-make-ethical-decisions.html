Businesses should train employees to make ethical decisions.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ethics training is essential for your company to successfully communicate and carry out its main goals. Effective ethics training for employees ensures your company is situated for success and that individuals within the company will stay committed to their tasks at hand in the best and most comfortable manner.

Concept note-2: -Business ethics enhances the law by outlining acceptable behaviors beyond government control. Corporations establish business ethics to promote integrity among their employees and gain trust from key stakeholders, such as investors and consumers.

Concept note-3: -Business Ethics is a code of conduct which society should follow while conducting their social activities.
