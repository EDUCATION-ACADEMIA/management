CUSTOMER SERVICE

[SOURCES]
What does the following statement mean? “Customer service is very inexpensive to initiate within a business, yet the absence of customer service will prove costly.”

(A) ** It will be costly if you lose loyal customers due to poor service

(B) it’s expensive to train employees so this is an invalid statement

(C) poor customer service increases profit potential

(D) good customer service decreases profit potential

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Customer service is the support you offer your customers-both before and after they buy and use your products or services-that helps them have an easy and enjoyable experience with you. Offering amazing customer service is important if you want to retain customers and grow your business.

Concept note-2: -Customer service is the interaction between the buyer of a product and the company that sells it. Good customer service is critical to business success, ensuring brand loyalty one customer at a time.

Concept note-3: -Good customer service means consistently meeting customers’ expectations. Great customer service is quick, easy, personalized, and empathetic. Companies that deliver excellent customer service take the time needed to understand the needs of their unique customer base.

Concept note-4: -Customer service is the support you offer your customers from the moment they first contact your business to the months and years afterward. Providing good customer service means being a reliable partner to your customers-it goes beyond helping them troubleshoot, use, and make informed decisions about your product.
