Which of the following is the definition of customer experience?

(A) The product of customer referrals.

(B) The product of upselling a customer.

(C) The product of an interaction between the organization’s employees.

(D) ** The product of an interaction between an organization and a customer.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Customer experience (CX) refers to how a business engages with its customers at every point of their buying journey-from marketing to sales to customer service and everywhere in between. In large part, it’s the sum total of all interactions a customer has with your brand.

Concept note-2: -Customer experience (CX) is all the interactions customers have with your company at all stages of the customer journey-even if it doesn’t result in a purchase. It focuses on the relationship between a business and its customers.

Concept note-3: -In truth, customer experience is best described as the perception customers have of your brand. Their perceptions are the reality of your CX, regardless of how well you think you are doing at delivering exceptional customer service, personalizing interactions, and meeting and exceeding their expectations.

Concept note-4: -Customer experience (CX) is everything related to a business that affects a customer’s perception and feelings about it. Customer experience (CX) focuses on the relationship between a business and its customers. It includes every interaction, no matter how brief and even if it doesn’t result in a purchase.

Concept note-5: -Discovery, Engagement, and Delivery are the three main components of Customer Experience.
