Raghib just started working for a new organization, and he asked the person training him “To whom do I go if I have a problem?” Raghib wants to understand the company’s

(A) peer pressure.

(B) organizational structure.

(C) ** chain of command.

(D) departmentalization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delegation of Authority (DOA) is the formal process in which one person delegates the authority and responsibility to another person to carry out specific activities.

Concept note-2: -Structure. the arrangement or relationship of positions within an organization.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: Formalization is one of the aspects of organizational structure. It refers to the extent to which formal rules, regulations, procedures, policies, and descriptions will be used to standardize the decisions and behaviors of the employees in the organization.

Concept note-4: -A matrix organization is defined as one in which there is dual or multiple managerial accountability and responsibility.
