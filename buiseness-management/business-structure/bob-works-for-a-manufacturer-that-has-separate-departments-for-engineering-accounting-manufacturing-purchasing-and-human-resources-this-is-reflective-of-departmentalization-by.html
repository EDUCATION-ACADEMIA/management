Bob works for a manufacturer that has separate departments for engineering, accounting, manufacturing, purchasing, and human resources. This is reflective of departmentalization by

(A) product

(B) osmosis

(C) ** function

(D) service

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Functional departmentalization organizes work and workers into separate units responsible for particular business functions or areas of expertise. Organizational structure is the vertical and horizontal configuration of departments, authority, and jobs within a company.

Concept note-2: -Divisional approach – where departments are formed based on management of their products, customers or geogprahic areas covered. Matrix approach – is a hybrid form of departmentatlization whre managers and staff personnel report to the superiors, the functional manager and the divisional manager.

Concept note-3: -Functional departmentalization organizes units by business activities such as finance, marketing, human resources, and production.

Concept note-4: -The correct option is B) Division of labor also sometimes called work specialization, means the division of tasks into separate jobs. Work specialization many occur to any degree.
