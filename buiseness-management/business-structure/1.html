BUISENESS MANAGEMENT

BUSINESS STRUCTURE

[SOURCES]
A reason why a firm should analyse its staffing structure would be to:

(A) Alter a marketing campaign

(B) Force staff to work harder

(C) Increase the range of products sold

(D) ** Identify gaps and vacancies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A gap analysis is a method of assessing the performance of a business unit to determine whether business requirements or objectives are being met and, if not, what steps should be taken to meet them. A gap analysis may also be referred to as a needs analysis, needs assessment or need-gap analysis.

Concept note-2: -A gap analysis in workforce planning is a process that looks at an organization’s workforce and its skills and compares it to the skills required to reach its key goals. It is used to identify potential deficiencies in an organization’s current workforce and abilities.

Concept note-3: -The three fundamental components of a gap analysis are the current state, desired state, and the gap. The current state is how business procedures and processes are being used. The desired state is where the company wants to be in the future. This usually includes improved efficiency and effectiveness.

Concept note-4: -Conducting a gap analysis can help you improve your business efficiency, your product, and your profitability by allowing you to pinpoint “gaps” present in your company. Once it’s complete, you’ll be able to better focus your resources and energy on those identified areas in order to improve them.
