What information can be included on an Organisation Chart

(A) ** Job Titles

(B) Date of Birth

(C) ** Names

(D) ** Room Numbers

(E) ** Photograph

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An organizational chart shows the internal structure of an organization or company. The employees and positions are represented by boxes or other shapes, sometimes including photos, contact information, email and page links, icons and illustrations. Straight or elbowed lines link the levels together.

Concept note-2: -An organizational chart is a diagram that visually conveys a company’s internal structure by detailing the roles, responsibilities, and relationships between individuals within an entity.

Concept note-3: -An organization chart of a company is the visual representation of it’s reporting structure. It defines the employee relationship hierarchy of an organization and the alignment matrix of teams. Org charts illustrates employee designations, reporting managers and more.
