Concept note-2: -This sourcebook identifies four major types of decentralization according to classifications made in UNDP and World Bank articles. These are political, administrative, fiscal, and market decentralization. Political decentralization is the transfer of authority to a subnational body.

Concept note-3: -Decentralization refers to organizational structures and procedures that allow for power sharing and delegation of decision-making authority to lower levels of the organizational hierarchy, including decision making by front line employees.
