Which describes the way of working whereby an employee can work from home, away from the office?

(A) Freelance

(B) Part-time

(C) ** Working whilst mobile

(D) Full-time

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In other words, telework implies work achieved with the help of ICTs and conducted outside the employer’s locations. Teleworking should happen in a voluntary agreement between the employer and the employee.

Concept note-2: -Virtual Worker/Workplace Initial terms describing remote work focused on using technology to work at a distance (”’tele” in Greek) from the office.

Concept note-3: -Telework is an arrangement between an employee and their employer which allows them to work away from the main office site. This may include working from home, a co-working space, or some other location.

Concept note-4: -Telework/Working Remotely/Telecommuting Telework or working remotely occurs when people do at least some of their regular work from home instead of going into the office or work at a different location.

Concept note-5: -Remote work is location-based. Being physically apart from your colleagues, leadership, and company office space is remote work. Virtual work is communicating, collaborating, and interacting in a virtual space. Remote work is disconnecting and disengaging.
