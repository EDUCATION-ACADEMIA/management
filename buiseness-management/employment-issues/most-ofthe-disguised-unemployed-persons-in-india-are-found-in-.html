BUISENESS MANAGEMENT

EMPLOYMENT ISSUES

[SOURCES]
Most ofthe disguised unemployed persons in India are found in-

(A) ** Agriculture

(B) Industry

(C) Trade

(D) Transport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is a & c. Disguised unemployment is primarily traced in the agricultural and unorganized sectors of the economy. As the primary sector of the Indian economy, Agriculture provides employment to almost 51% of the total population.

Concept note-2: -Disguised unemployment is mostly found in agriculture sector in rural areas in India where 10 members of the farmers family are employed on small plot when 5 are enough. Hence, correct answer is option B.

Concept note-3: -These migrants join them and claim to be employed in farming. This additional labour does not add much to output and is therefore considered to be mostly disguised unemployment.

Concept note-4: -Disguised unemployment is mostly found in the tertiary sector.

Concept note-5: -Disguised unemployment is found in the agricultural sector of India. Disguised unemployment is also known as hidden unemployment. In this type of unemployment, some people seem employed but they are actually not.
