What are the benefits to an employee of workplace training

(A) there are no benefits, it is a waste of time.

(B) the employer gets all the benefits

(C) ** you may get higher pay and promotions

(D) a day off work to go to a course

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A well trained employee usually shows greater productivity and higher quality of work-output than an untrained employee. Training increases the skills of the employees in the performance of a particular job. An increase in the skills usually helps to increase both quantity and quality of output.

Concept note-2: -Upskilling is a great way to show your employees that you care about their progression and make them feel there’s something ‘in it for them’, which, in turn, boosts employee productivity, satisfaction, and retention rates within an organization.

Concept note-3: -(i) Better Career Opportunities: Training enhances the skill and knowledge of the employees and thereby, helps in improving their career prospects. (ii) Earn More: By helping the individual to learn more it assist them to earn more. Training enhances the individuals understanding and knowledge.
