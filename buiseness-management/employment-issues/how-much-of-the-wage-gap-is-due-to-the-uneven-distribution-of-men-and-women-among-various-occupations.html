How much of the wage gap is due to the uneven distribution of men and women among various occupations?

(A) two-thirds

(B) one-fourth

(C) ** one-third

(D) two-fourths

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All women were paid, on average, 83% of what men were paid.

Concept note-2: -According to the estimates of the World Inequality Report 2022, in India, men earn 82 per cent of the labour income whereas women earn 18 per cent of it.

Concept note-3: -In 2020, women who were full-time wage and salary workers had median usual weekly earnings that were 82 percent of those of male full-time wage and salary workers. In 1979, the first year for which comparable earnings data are available, women’s earnings were 62 percent of men’s.

Concept note-4: -Overall, women in the United States are paid 77 cents for every dollar paid to men, and that gap is widest for women of color.
