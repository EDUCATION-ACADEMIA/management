Who is at risk of unethical marketing tactics?

(A) ** all internet users

(B) business executives

(C) marketing managers

(D) the average consumer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The primary risks are as follows: Brand/Image-Unethical marketing affects our brand image in the eyes of consumers. Fines-The government levies fines from breaches of consumer protection laws and unfair trade practices. Also, private individuals can bring lawsuits based on unethical marketing practices.

Concept note-2: -Here are some common unethical marketing practices to be aware of: Misleading advertising. Contacting people without their consent. Inciting controversy.

Concept note-3: -Some examples of unethical advertising include misleading advertisements, discriminatory advertising, making false health claims, and using fear tactics in ads.
