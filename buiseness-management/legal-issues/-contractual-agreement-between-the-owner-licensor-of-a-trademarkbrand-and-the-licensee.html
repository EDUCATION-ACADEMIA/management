____ :Contractual agreement between the owner (licensor) of a trademark/brand and the licensee.

(A) ** licensing

(B) public domain

(C) intellectual property

(D) collective bargaining

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An agreement of license between a trademark owner and a manufacturer is an official document that states that the manufacturer of a product has the permission to manufacture the product by the company or the individual who has trademarked it.

Concept note-2: -What is trademark licensing? (1) Definition. In trademark licensing, a trademark owner (Licensor) grants permission to another (Licensee) to use that trademark on mutually agreed terms and conditions.

Concept note-3: -This contract is used when a company (Licensor) assigns or licenses to another (Licensee) registered industrial and intellectual property rights (patents, utility models, trademarks, copyright etc.) as well as technical assistance and know-how.

Concept note-4: -A licensing agreement is a legal contract between two parties, known as the licensor and the licensee.
