A good contract will spell out ____, or unforeseeable “acts of God” beyond anyone’s control, for which neither party is responsible.

(A) ** contingencies

(B) liabilities

(C) amendments

(D) disasters

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A service mark distinguishes the provider of a service rather than a product.

Concept note-2: -The answer is earnest money. The essential elements of a valid contract are offer and acceptance, consideration, legally competent parties, mutual agreement, and legal purpose. Earnest money is not required for a valid contract.

Concept note-3: -Definition. A limited liability company (LLC) is a type of business form combining attributes of both corporations and partnerships. It has TWO PRIMARY ATTRIBUTES: (1) the limited liability that shareholders of a corporation enjoy, AND. (2) the tax treatment of a partnership.
