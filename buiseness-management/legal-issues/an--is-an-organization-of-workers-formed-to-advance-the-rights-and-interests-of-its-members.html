A(n) ____ is an organization of workers formed to advance the rights and interests of its members.

(A) strike

(B) ** labor union

(C) lockout

(D) committee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Labor unions are associations of workers formed to protect workersâ€™ rights and advance their interests. Unions negotiate with employers through a process known as collective bargaining.

Concept note-2: -Labor Organization refers to any union or association of employees in the private sector which exists in whole or in part for the purpose of collective bargaining or of dealing with employers concerning terms and conditions of employment. It is classified into FORMAL and INFORMAL sector labor organization.

Concept note-3: -A labor union is a group of two or more employees who join together to advance common interests such as wages, benefits, schedules and other employment terms and conditions.

Concept note-4: -trade union, also called labour union, association of workers in a particular trade, industry, or company created for the purpose of securing improvements in pay, benefits, working conditions, or social and political status through collective bargaining.
