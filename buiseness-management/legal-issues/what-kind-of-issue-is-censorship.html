what kind of issue is censorship?

(A) ** Ethical

(B) Environmental

(C) Legal

(D) BIG

(E) It’s no issue at all, people shouldn’t be allowed to read about opinions other than mine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Censorship, the suppression of words, images, or ideas that are “offensive, ” happens whenever some people succeed in imposing their personal political or moral values on others. Censorship can be carried out by the government as well as private pressure groups. Censorship by the government is unconstitutional.

Concept note-2: -Moral censorship is the removal of materials that are obscene or otherwise considered morally questionable. Pornography, for example, is often censored under this rationale, especially child pornography, which is illegal and censored in most jurisdictions in the world.

Concept note-3: -An ethical issue is a problem or situation that requires a person to choose between two options, where both options are morally wrong. Ethical issues can be divided into three categories: personal, professional, and social.

Concept note-4: -Censorship is used to control the social interaction of the public with the media. Such a censorship can be directed to various medias if the media is judged to be either immoral, blasphemous, obscene, unpatriotic, radical or seditious.
