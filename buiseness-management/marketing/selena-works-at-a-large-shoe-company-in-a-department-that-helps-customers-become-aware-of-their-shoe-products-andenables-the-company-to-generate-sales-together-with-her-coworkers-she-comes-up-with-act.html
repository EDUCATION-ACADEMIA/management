Selena works at a large shoe company in a department that helps customers become aware of their shoe products andenables the company to generate sales. Together with her coworkers, she comes up with activities such as runningadvertising campaigns on social media, setting up billboards, or sponsoring events. What department does Selena most likely work in?

(A) distribution

(B) ** marketing

(C) human resources

(D) finance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Sales Concept focuses on the needs of the seller. The Marketing Concept focuses on the needs of the buyer.

Concept note-2: -The 7Ps of marketing are – product, pricing, place, promotion, physical evidence, people, and processes. The 7 Ps make up the necessary marketing mix that a business must have to advertise a product or service.
