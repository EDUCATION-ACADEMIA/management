MARKETING

[SOURCES]
Needs of ____ are being focused in Marketing.

(A) ** Customer

(B) producer

(C) Retailer

(D) wholesaler

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Customer focused marketing is the process of determining customer needs and wants in order to drive the working force behind the company’s products or services. Managers in the company must determine key factors about its customers in order to steer its marketing approach.

Concept note-2: -Being customer focused puts you in a better position to help your customers, in an honest way. Your customer will sense and see that you are making an extra effort to understand the situation at which they are and to really understand them to be better able to help them get where they want to be.

Concept note-3: -What are customer needs? Customer needs are the psychological and physical motivations that make someone want to purchase a product or service and stay loyal to that business. For example, customers today need quick and convenient ways to reach support online.

Concept note-4: -The Marketing Concept focuses on the needs of the buyer. 2. The Sales Concept is preoccupied with the seller’s need to convert his/her product into cash. The Marketing Concept is preoccupied with the idea of satisfying the needs of the customer by means of the product as a solution to the customer’s problem (needs).
