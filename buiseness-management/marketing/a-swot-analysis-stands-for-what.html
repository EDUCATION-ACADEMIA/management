Concept note-1: -SWOT analysis is a framework for identifying and analyzing an organizationâ€™s strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats. These words make up the SWOT acronym. Concept note-2: -SWOT (strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats) analysis is a method for identifying and analyzing internal strengths and weaknesses and external opportunities and threats that shape current and future operations and help develop strategic goals. SWOT analyses are not limited to companies.

Concept note-3: -SWOT stands for Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats, and so a SWOT analysis is a technique for assessing these four aspects of your business.
