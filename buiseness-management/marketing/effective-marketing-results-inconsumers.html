Effective marketing results in:consumers

(A) ** lower prices of products and services for consumers

(B) higher prices in the long run

(C) higher costs and lower profits

(D) lower sales volume and greater competition

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It helps to establish linkages between the business, accelerating economic activity, leading to higher incomes, more consumption, increased savings and investment.

Concept note-2: -If a consumer believes they are getting a good deal, then lower prices can help get you the sale. On the other hand, low prices can also give the impression that the product is of low quality.

Concept note-3: -Marketing campaigns can influence consumer behaviors because they elicit reactions, utilizing imagery and word associations tied to emotional responses. This article is for professionals and entrepreneurs who want to improve their persuasive ability in sales and business relationships.

Concept note-4: -Penetration pricing is a marketing strategy used by businesses to attract customers to a new product or service by offering a lower price during its initial offering.
