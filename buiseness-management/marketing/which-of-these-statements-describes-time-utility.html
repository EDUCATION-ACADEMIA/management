EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Product mix, also known as product assortment or product portfolio, refers to the complete set of products and/or services offered by a firm.

Concept note-2: -A distribution channel, in simple terms, is the flow that a good or service follows from production or manufacturing to the final consumer/buyer. Distribution channels vary but typically include a producer, a wholesaler, a retailer, and the end buyer/consumer.

Concept note-3: -The product. The competitors. What is the term for the path that the product follows from the producer to the customer? Physical distribution.

Concept note-4: -The maturity stage is when the sales begin to level off from the rapid growth period. At this point, companies begin to reduce their prices so they can stay competitive amongst the growing competition.
