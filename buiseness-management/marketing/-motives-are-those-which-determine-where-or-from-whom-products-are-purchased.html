____ motives are those which determine where or from whom products are purchased.

(A) Product.

(B) ** Patronage.

(C) Emotional.

(D) Rational.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Buying motive is the urge or motive to satisfy a desire or need that makes people buy goods or services. Behind every purchase there is a buying motive. ADVERTISEMENTS: It refers to the thoughts, feelings, emotions and instincts, which arouse in the buyers a desire to buy an article.

Concept note-2: -Patronage motives are based on loyalty and encourage consumers to purchase from a particular business or to buy a particular brand. Loyalty is influenced by positive previous experiences or a close identification with the product or business.

Concept note-3: -Rational patronage buying motives: When a buyer decides to patronize or purchase from a certain shop or seller after carefully or logically considering each and every aspect associated with it. 5.8.
