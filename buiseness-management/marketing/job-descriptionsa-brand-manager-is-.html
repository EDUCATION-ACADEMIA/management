Job descriptions:A Brand Manager is ____

(A) In charge of collecting information from costumers

(B) In charge of the public relations and publicity of a company

(C) ** In charge of developing and executing brand strategies and marketing initiatives

(D) In charge of creating advertising campaings in media

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Brand Managers are responsible for ensuring that the products, services and product lines that fall under their domain resonate with current and potential customers. To do so, these professionals continuously monitor marketing trends and keep a close eye on competitive products in the marketplace.

Concept note-2: -The role of a Brand Manager is to develop a brand strategy for a company. Brand managers oversee a wide array of business functions including branding, communication channels, product development, online and offline promotions, and market research. They often conduct market research for a company.

Concept note-3: -They work closely with the marketing, advertising and sales departments to provide a sense of direction for their brand. Their job is to use their marketing and industry expertise to set budgets for campaigns, organize promotional events for products and encourage brand uniformity across marketing initiatives.
