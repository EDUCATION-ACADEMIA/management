(A) SEM (search engine marketing)

(B) ** SEO (search engine optimization)

(C) SERP (search engine results page)

(D) Link building

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SEO stands for “search engine optimization.” In simple terms, SEO means the process of improving your website to increase its visibility in Google, Microsoft Bing, and other search engines whenever people search for: Products you sell.

Concept note-2: -It helps consumers make a personal connection with your brand and fosters customer loyalty with those who already buy from you. But it doesn’t even come close to SEO when it comes to driving traffic to your website. The average SEO campaign generates 1000 percent more traffic than the average social media campaign.

Concept note-3: -White Hat SEO It’s also known as good SEO, because you rely on the terms presented by the search engine. This improves your SERPs rankings.

Concept note-4: -This way, you can build your own strategy and implement the recommendations provided to grow your website traffic. SEO is a long-term digital marketing strategy that requires long-haul energy for brainstorming and experimenting in order to achieve the best results, organically.
