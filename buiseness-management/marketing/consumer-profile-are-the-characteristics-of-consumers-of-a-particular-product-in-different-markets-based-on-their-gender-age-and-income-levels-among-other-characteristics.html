Consumer profile are the characteristics of consumers of a particular product in different markets based on their gender, age, and income levels, among other characteristics.

(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A customer profile can be based on a number of identifiers including demographics, location, hobbies, preferred social media channels, likes/dislikes, buying patterns, psychographics and credit background.

Concept note-2: -What are Consumer Profiles? A consumer profile is a compilation of consumer information, including demographic, geographic, psychographic, and behavioral data, to create a detailed understanding of consumers for marketing and research purposes.

Concept note-3: -Consumer market characteristics can be divided into demographic, geographical, psychographic, and behavioristic traits. Demographic characteristics include age, religion, gender, nationality, and race. Geographical characteristics include region, population density, and market size.
