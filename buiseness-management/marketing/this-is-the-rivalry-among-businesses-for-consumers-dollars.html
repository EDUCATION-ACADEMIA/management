This is the rivalry among businesses for consumers’ dollars.

(A) Marketing

(B) ** Competition

(C) Personal Selling

(D) Visual Merchandising

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Price Competition-A type of rivalry between or among businesses that focuses on the use of price to attract scarce customer dollars.

Concept note-2: -Competition. the rivalry among businesses for consumers’ dollars, is another vital element in free enterprise.

Concept note-3: -competition. the rivalry among businesses to sell their goods and services to buyers. demand.

Concept note-4: -[1] When only a single firm sells a product or service for which there is no substitute, the firm is a monopoly. [2] When only a single firm buys a product or service, the firm is a monopsony. A monopsony can exist in labor markets, when there is only one employer in a market.

Concept note-5: -Communism: Society in which people, without regard to class, own all the nation’s resources.
