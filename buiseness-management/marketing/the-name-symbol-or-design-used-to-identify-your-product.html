The name, symbol, or design used to identify your product

(A) ** Brand

(B) Target Market

(C) Logo

(D) Advertisement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A brand is a name, term, sign or symbol, design or some combination of them is used to identify the products.

Concept note-2: -Trademarks identify exclusive ownership over a brand and/or product, along with any associated marketing tools. Registering trademarks prevent others from using your products or services without obtaining your permission.

Concept note-3: -Brand symbols (colloquially called “logos") are visual characteristics of a brand. They represent the brand’s personality and contribute to its recognition rate. The personality of a brand is determined by its content (brand core values) and its style (brand style), which must express these brand core values.

Concept note-4: -A brand is a name, term, design, symbol or any other feature that identifies one seller’s goods or service as distinct from those of other sellers.

Concept note-5: -The Label identifies the product or brand.
