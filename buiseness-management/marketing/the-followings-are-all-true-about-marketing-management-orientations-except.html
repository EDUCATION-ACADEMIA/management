MARKETING

[SOURCES]
The followings are all true about marketing management orientations except

(A) Production concept is the idea that consumers will favor products that are available or highly affordable

(B) Product concept is the idea that consumers will favor products that offer the most quality, performance, and features. Organization should therefore devote its energy to making continuous product improvements.

(C) Selling concept is the idea that consumers will not buy enough of the firm’s products unless it undertakes a large scale selling and promotion effort

(D) ** Societal concept is the idea that achieving organizational goals depends on knowing the needs and wants of the target markets and delivering the desired satisfactions better than competitors do

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marketing orientation is the business approach that dictates all the processes within that organization. It comes in several types: sales orientation, market orientation, production orientation, and societal orientation.

Concept note-2: -The societal marketing concept adopts the position that marketers have a greater social responsibility than simply satisfying customers and providing them with superior value. Instead, marketing activities should strive to benefit society’s overall well-being.

Concept note-3: -An organisation focus (and subsequently its marketing) is centred around five key categories, classified into the following orientation groups: Production orientation, product orientation, sales orientation, societal orientation and market orientation.

Concept note-4: -Solution(By Examveda Team) Focus of advertising is on product features and how products are made is NOT consistent with a manager having a marketing orientation.
