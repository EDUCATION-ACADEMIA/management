It is traditional approach in marketing which focuses on enticing consumers to purchase goods or services.

(A) Product approach

(B) Production approach

(C) ** Sales approach

(D) Societal approach

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is traditional approach in marketing which focuses on enticing consumers to purchase goods or services. It is a promotional tool which uses the sales skills to build a personal relationship with customers.

Concept note-2: -Traditional marketing relies on offline strategies, including direct sales, direct mail (postcards, brochures, letters, fliers), tradeshows, print advertising (magazines, newspapers, coupon books, billboards), referral (also known as word-of-mouth marketing), radio, and television.

Concept note-3: -Traditional marketing channels include: Outdoor (Billboards, bus/taxi wraps, posters etc) Broadcasting (TV, Radio etc) Print (Magazines, newspapers etc)
