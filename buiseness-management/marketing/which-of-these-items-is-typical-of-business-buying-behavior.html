Which of these items is typical of business buying behavior?

(A) More likely to negotiate on price

(B) Has more frequent contact with the seller

(C) Commonly buys in larger amounts

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A distribution channel, in simple terms, is the flow that a good or service follows from production or manufacturing to the final consumer/buyer. Distribution channels vary but typically include a producer, a wholesaler, a retailer, and the end buyer/consumer.

Concept note-2: -The business-to-business (B2B) market includes people who buy products to: Resell them, Use them in day-to-day operations, Make other products.

Concept note-3: -What is the main reason why generic products can be sold at a reduced price? Less money is spent to promote them. What is the difference between a customer and a consumer? A customer buys products, while a consumer uses products.

Concept note-4: -Identifying a target market allows marketers to focus on those most likely to purchase the product. Limiting the population funnels research and budgets to the customers with the highest profit potential.
