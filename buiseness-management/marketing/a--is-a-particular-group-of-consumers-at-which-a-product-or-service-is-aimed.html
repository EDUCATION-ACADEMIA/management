A ____ is a particular group of consumers at which a product or service is aimed.

(A) ** target market

(B) differentiated offering

(C) pro forma

(D) patent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A target market is a group of people that have been identified as the most likely potential customers for a product because of their shared characteristics such as age, income, and lifestyle.

Concept note-2: -A target market is the group of people most likely to purchase a specific product or service. Itâ€™s important for businesses to understand their target market, because members of this group are most likely to buy and recommend their products, improving the number of sales.

Concept note-3: -A target market is a group of potential customers that you identify to sell products or services to. Each group can be divided into smaller segments. Segments are typically grouped by age, location, income and lifestyle.

Concept note-4: -The four target markets are geographic, demographic, psychographic, and behavioral.
