A(n) ____ is a proposal to provide a good or service.

(A) ** offer

(B) request

(C) discussion

(D) contract

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An offer is a clear proposal to sell or buy a specific product or service under specific conditions. Offers are made in a manner that a reasonable person would understand acceptance and will result in a binding contract.

Concept note-2: -What is a service proposal? A service proposal is a document that you use to pitch your company’s services and solutions to potential clients. It acts as a sales proposal, demonstrating that you understand your new clients’ problems and you know how to solve them.

Concept note-3: -Meaning of offer or proposal: – When one person signifies his willingness to do or to abstain from doing something with a view of obtaining the assent of others, he is said to make an offer or proposal. The term ‘Offer or Proposal’ has been defined in Section 2(a) of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

Concept note-4: -A business proposal is an official pitch to clients selling your products or services. A business proposal outlines a particular product or service offered by an established business to a prospective client. You’re trying to sell your prospective client on your product or service, not on your business itself.
