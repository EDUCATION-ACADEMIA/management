How is a motel positioning itself when it emphasizes a good value and low rates?

(A) ** By price

(B) By quality

(C) By location

(D) By amenities

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The primary purpose of hotels is to provide travelers with shelter, food, refreshment, and similar services and goods, offering on a commercial basis things that are customarily furnished within households but unavailable to people on a journey away from home.

Concept note-2: -Which is the best example that demonstrates the concept of suggestive selling in the hospitality industry? A resort reservationist asks guests if they would prefer to book a suite instead of a king single room.

Concept note-3: -Sales promotion techniques help in attracting new guests or customers. To attract a guest, one must first stimulate interest and a desire to act. The targeted promotion helps in increasing awareness and then turning that awareness into consumer purchasing action.

Concept note-4: -The accommodation sector is central to the travel and hospitality industry, because people travelling to different areas require somewhere to stay, rest, sleep and unwind. In fact, by many definitions, a tourist is only classed as such if their stay exceeds 24 hours and they use some form of overnight accommodation.
