What is the term for a facility used by wholesalers or retailers for the receipt, temporary storage, and redistribution of goods according to the customer orders as they are received?

(A) A warehouse

(B) ** A distribution center

(C) A piggy-back service

(D) A telemarketing facility

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BusinessDictionary.com defines a distribution center as a facility that is used for receipt, temporary storage and redistribution of goods according to orders as they are received. A distribution center serves as a bridge between the supplier and its customers.

Concept note-2: -A distribution center is a warehouse or storage facility where the emphasis is on processing and moving goods on to wholesalers, retailers, or consumers1.

Concept note-3: -Distribution channels include wholesalers, retailers, distributors, and the Internet. In a direct distribution channel, the manufacturer sells directly to the consumer.

Concept note-4: -The main difference between a distributor and a distribution center is that a distributor is a type of business, while a distribution center is a business facility. Distributors typically operate their primary business activities out of a distribution center, but other types of businesses also use distribution centers.
