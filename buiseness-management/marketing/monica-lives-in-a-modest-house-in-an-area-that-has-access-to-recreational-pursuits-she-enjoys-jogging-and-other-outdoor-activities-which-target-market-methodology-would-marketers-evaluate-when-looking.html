MARKETING

[SOURCES]
Monica lives in a modest house in an area that has access to recreational pursuits. She enjoys jogging and other outdoor activities. Which target market methodology would marketers evaluate when looking at Monica’s lifestyle?

(A) Demographics

(B) Geographics

(C) ** Psychographics

(D) Behaviorist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Demographic, psychographic, behavioral and geographic segmentation are considered the four main types of market segmentation, but there are also many other strategies you can use, including numerous variations on the four main types.

Concept note-2: -Dividing a target market into segments means grouping the population according to the key characteristics that drive their spending decisions. Some of these are gender, age, income level, race, education level, religion, marital status, and geographic location.

Concept note-3: -The four target markets are geographic, demographic, psychographic, and behavioral.

Concept note-4: -Demographic segmentation is a precise form of audience identification based on data points like age, gender, marital status, family size, income, education, race, occupation, nationality, and/or religion. It’s among the four main types of marketing segmentation, and perhaps the most commonly used method.
