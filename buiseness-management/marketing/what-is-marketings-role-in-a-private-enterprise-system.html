What is Marketing’s Role in a Private Enterprise System?

(A) Marketing fits into every facet of our lives, whether on a global scale or right in our own neighborhoods

(B) Provides benefits that make our lives better, promoting using natural resources more wisely, and encourage international trade

(C) Without marketing, we would all have to be self-sufficient

(D) ** all of above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marketing’s role in a private enterprise system allows for you to be aware of, have access to, and the ability to purchase the goods and services you may want/need.

Concept note-2: -It serves as the face of your company, coordinating and producing all materials representing the business. It is the Marketing Department’s job to reach out to prospects, customers, investors and/or the community, while creating an overarching image that represents your company in a positive light.

Concept note-3: -Build awareness about a brand. Grow a company’s market share. Innovate and launch new services and products or pivot from existing models.
