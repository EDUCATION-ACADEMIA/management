[SOURCES]
Evaluating customer acceptance is ____ in product development.

(A) not included

(B) the first step

(C) not important

(D) ** the last step

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The final stage of your new product development process is commercialization, where you introduce your products to market.

Concept note-2: -The final stage in the new product development process is commercialisation. Commercialisation means nothing else than INTRODUCING A NEW PRODUCT INTO THE MARKET.

Concept note-3: -Although the product development process differs by industry, it can essentially be broken down into seven stages: ideation, research, planning, prototyping, sourcing, costing, and commercialization.
