Data that describes a group of people in terms of their age, marital status, family size, ethnicity, gender, profession, education, and income.

(A) Geographics

(B) Psychographics

(C) ** Demographics

(D) Primary Data

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Demographic-or more broadly, sociodemographic-statistics refer to characteristics of a population, such as age, race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, income, education, and marital status.

Concept note-2: -Demographics are data that describe a group of people in terms of their age, marital status, family size, ethnicity, gender, profession, education, and income.

Concept note-3: -Demographic segmentation is a precise form of audience identification based on data points like age, gender, marital status, family size, income, education, race, occupation, nationality, and/or religion. It’s among the four main types of marketing segmentation, and perhaps the most commonly used method.

Concept note-4: -Data that describe a group of people in terms of their tastes, opinions, personality traits, and lifestyle habits are called demographics.
