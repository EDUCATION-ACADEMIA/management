MARKETING

[SOURCES]
Process of gathering, analyzing, and interpreting information about market, product, or service to be offered for sale in the market, about the past, present and potential customers for the product or service.

(A) MARKETING PLAN

(B) ** MARKET RESEARCH

(C) MARKETING STRATEGY

(D) PRODUCT TESTING

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Market research, also known as “marketing research, ” is the process of determining the viability of a new service or product through research conducted directly with potential customers.

Concept note-2: -Marketing researchThe process of collecting, analyzing, and reporting marketing information that can be used to improve a company’s bottom line. is the process of collecting, analyzing, and reporting marketing information that can be used to answer questions or solve problems so as to improve a company’s bottom line.

Concept note-3: -Market research is the process of gathering and analyzing information about a market, product, or service in order to gain insight into customers, competitors, and the market itself.

Concept note-4: -Market research is the process of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data about competitors, target markets, or an industry as a whole.
