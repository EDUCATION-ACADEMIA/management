Apple does research to determine the best packaging for protecting the new IPhone. What type of research is this?

(A) ** Product

(B) Attitude

(C) Sales

(D) Media

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For many Apple products such as the iPhone and the MacBook, Apple packages these in premium, full cover rigid boxes with a separate lid and base. One thing many people notice first is how the lid slowly and smoothly glides off the base when opening the box.

Concept note-2: -We have already reduced plastic in our packaging by 65 percent since 2015. And since 2017, 100 percent of the virgin wood fiber used in our packaging has come from responsible sources. This goal applies only to primary and secondary packaging of Apple products.

Concept note-3: -Nearly 20 percent of all material used in Apple products in 2021 was recycled, the highest-ever use of recycled content. Apple released new details on this progress, its recycling innovation efforts, and clean energy in its 2022 Environmental Progress Report.

Concept note-4: -The iPhone box is made from paper, which can be recycled just about anywhere.
