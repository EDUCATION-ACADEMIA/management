Which of the following is not a suitable factors to consider when you’re trying to increase CTR across different digital marketing channels

(A) ** Remove Call to Action

(B) Optimise your headline and copy

(C) Use images

(D) Try using hashtags

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Many factors affect click-through rate (CTR), including imagery, keywords, call-to-action copy and ad positioning. Click through rate compares the number of time someone clicks on your content, to the number of impressions you get (how many times the ad was viewed).

Concept note-2: -Clickthrough rate (CTR) can be used to gauge how well your keywords and ads, and free listings, are performing. CTR is the number of clicks that your ad receives divided by the number of times your ad is shown: clicks ÷ impressions = CTR. For example, if you had 5 clicks and 100 impressions, then your CTR would be 5%.
