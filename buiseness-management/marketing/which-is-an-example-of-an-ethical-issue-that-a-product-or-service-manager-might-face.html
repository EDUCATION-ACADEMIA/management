Which is an example of an ethical issue that a product or service manager might face?

(A) Use of color on the label

(B) ** Use of environmentally friendly packaging

(C) Use of packaging as a means of promotion

(D) Use of nutrition information on a food label

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marketing harmful products to consumers are highly unethical. It manipulates customers’ decisions by tricking them into buying the product which may have adverse effects on their health.

Concept note-2: -Change promotion– Different advertising or sales promotion techniques can prolong the life of the product, giving it a new image.

Concept note-3: -Product Line: A group of closely related products manufactured or sold by a business. Product Item: A specific model, brand, or size of a product within a product line.
