(A) Bank savings account

(B) Government bonds

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What are the Different Types of Interest? The three types of interest include simple (regular) interest, accrued interest, and compounding interest.

Concept note-2: -Traditional or Regular Savings Account These are the savings accounts you typically find at traditional banks or credit unions. These types of savings accounts generally allow you to earn interest on your money, although they usually pay lower rates than other savings products.
