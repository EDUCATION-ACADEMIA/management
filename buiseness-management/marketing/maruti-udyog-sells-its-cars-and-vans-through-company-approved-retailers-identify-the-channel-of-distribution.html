Maruti Udyog sells its cars and vans through company approved retailers. Identify the channel of distribution.

(A) ** One Level

(B) Two Level

(C) Three Level

(D) Zero Level

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indirect channel (one level of distribution channel).

Concept note-2: -One-level channel Manufacturer->Retailer-> Consumer. Two-level channel Manufacturer-> Wholesaler-> Retailer->Consumer. Three-level channel Manufacturer->Agent->Wholesaler-> Retailer Consumer.

Concept note-3: -Channel of distribution means all the people who come together in helping and assisting in transfer of the title of goods and services from the producer to the consumer. These people are the middlemen such as wholesalers, retailers, agents, merchants, institutions etc.

Concept note-4: -Maruti Suzuki India Limited (MSIL), formerly known as Maruti Udyog Limited, a subsidiary of Suzuki Motor Corporation of Japan, is Indiaâ€™s largest passenger car company. It is an example of joint sector.

Concept note-5: -Joint sector industries are owned and operated by the state and individuals or a group of individuals. Maruti Udyog Limited is an example of joint sector.
