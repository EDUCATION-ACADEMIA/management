What is an advantage of using social media as advertising?

(A) ** It can be free/cheap

(B) It appeals to only a younger audience

(C) It is easy to get right

(D) YouTube

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thankfully, social media allows for easy and effective brand building. Social media has a benefit over traditional media because it can get your brand in front of people much more quickly and easily. Furthermore, it gets your audience looking at your brand even when they aren’t thinking about your brand or product.

Concept note-2: -Social media marketing is cost effective. It’s also one of the most versatile strategies that small businesses can use to reach their targeted audience as well as boost sales over a particular period of time.

Concept note-3: -Which social media is cheapest to advertise on? Actually, out of the four social media platforms, Twitter has the lowest cost per click at $0.38, and Facebook isn’t far behind at $0.97.

Concept note-4: -With ads, you can put your content in front of them with the frequency and budget you control. More Website or In-Person Traffic – Increased traffic to your website is another benefit of paid social advertising, which can help you boost conversions on your website pages.
