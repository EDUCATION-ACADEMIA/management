Mass marketing involves identifying market segments, selecting one or more of them, and developing products and marketing programs tailored to each.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -7) Mass marketing involves identifying market segments, selecting one or more of them, and developing products and marketing programs tailored to each.

Concept note-2: -Market segmentation is an extension of market research that seeks to identify targeted groups of consumers to tailor products and branding in a way that is attractive to the group.

Concept note-3: -In Segemetation, Mass marketing refers to the strategy of targeting the entire potential customer market by means of a single marketing message. The marketing strategy used in this segmentation does not target the specific requirements or needs of customers.

Concept note-4: -Market segmentation strategy In a differentiated approach the firm targets one or more market segments, and develops separate offers for each segment. In consumer marketing, it is difficult to find examples of undifferentiated approaches.
