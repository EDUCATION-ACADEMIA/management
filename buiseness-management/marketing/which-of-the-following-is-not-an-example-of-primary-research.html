Which of the following is NOT an example of primary research?

(A) Focus Groups

(B) Questinnaires

(C) Observations

(D) ** Newspapers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: The answer is: d. The U.S. Census.

Concept note-2: -Newspapers may be either primary or secondary. Most articles in newspapers are secondary, but reporters may be considered as witnesses to an event. Any topic on the media coverage of an event or phenomenon would treat newspapers as a primary source.

Concept note-3: -Newspaper and Magazine Articles. Articles from magazines and newspapers from the time of an event are another type of primary source.
