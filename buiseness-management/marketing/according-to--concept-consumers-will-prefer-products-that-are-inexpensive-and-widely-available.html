According to ____ concept, consumers will prefer products that are inexpensive and widely available.

(A) Exchange

(B) ** Production

(C) Selling

(D) Marketing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This concept is the oldest of the concepts in business. It holds that consumers will prefer products that are widely available and inexpensive. Managers focusing on this concept concentrate on achieving high production efficiency, low costs, and mass distribution.

Concept note-2: -Production concept of marketing It speaks to the human truth that we prefer products that are easily available and inexpensive.

Concept note-3: -The selling concept is all about what the company wants or needs, but the marketing concept focuses on what the customer wants.

Concept note-4: -The Product concept holds that consumers will favour those products that offer the most quality, performance, or innovative features. The product concept proposes that consumers will prefer products that have better quality, performance and features as opposed to a normal product.
