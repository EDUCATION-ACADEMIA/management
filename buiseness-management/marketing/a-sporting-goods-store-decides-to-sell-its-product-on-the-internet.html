A sporting goods store decides to sell its product on the internet.

(A) Distribution

(B) Pricing

(C) Information Management

(D) ** Selling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is (b) distribution The distribution marketing function determines the best ways for customers to check what product is available for them and at what location.

Concept note-2: -Direct marketing consists of any marketing that relies on direct communication or distribution to individual consumers, rather than through a third party such as mass media.

Concept note-3: -Intensive distribution is a level of distribution density whereby a firm tries to place its products and services in as many outlets as possible.

Concept note-4: -Product/Service Management helps businesses to decide on which products to produce or sell.
