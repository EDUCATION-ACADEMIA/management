Which is the order of the research process?

(A) collect data; analyze the problem; design the research; evaluation of the plan; define the sample

(B) ** define the problem; design the research; collect data; analyze data; make recommendations for a plan of action; evaluation of plan

(C) analyze data; act on the plan; design the research; evaluation of the plan

(D) act; analyze; evaluation; define; plan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The research design involves the following steps: Step 1: Conduct secondary data analysis. Step 2: Do qualitative research. Step 3: Determine methods of collecting quantitative data (survey, observation, and experimentation) Step 4: Determine the definition of the information needed.

Concept note-2: -Research is a dynamic process that can be organized into four stages: Exploring, Investigating, Processing, and Creating.
