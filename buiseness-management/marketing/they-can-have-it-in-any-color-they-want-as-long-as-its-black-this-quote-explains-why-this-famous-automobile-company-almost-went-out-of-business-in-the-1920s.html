They can have it in any color they want, as long as its black.” This quote explains why this famous automobile company almost went out of business in the 1920’s.

(A) ** The Ford Motor Company

(B) The John Deere Company

(C) The Chrysler Corporation

(D) General Motors

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One of Henry Ford’s famous quotes about the Model T was, “Any customer can have a car painted any colour that he wants, so long as it is black.” The Model T only came in black because the production line required compromise so that efficiency and improved quality could be achieved.

Concept note-2: -Henry Ford is credited as once saying about his cars, You can have it in any color, as long as its black.

Concept note-3: -Production era The situation continued as a result of the First and Second World Wars where disruption to the supply chain and rationing of basic items became a way of life. As Henry Ford once said: “Any customer can have a car painted any color he wants, so long as it’s black.”

Concept note-4: -"There is no failure except failure to serve one’s purpose."

Concept note-5: -If people tell you they want faster horses, they are telling you something useful. Namely, that they have a desire to go faster. It is the desired outcome (’going faster’) that you should focus on, not the perceived solution (’horses’).
