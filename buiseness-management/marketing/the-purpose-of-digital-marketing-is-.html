The purpose of Digital Marketing is ____

(A) To encourage users to use mobile devices like mobile phones and mobile tablets

(B) To save time and effort in marketing

(C) ** Leverage on digital platform to promote and sell goods and services

(D) To protect the environment as print media is bad for the environment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A digital marketing strategy allows you to leverage different digital channels–such as social media, pay-per-click, search engine optimization, and email marketing–to connect with existing customers and individuals interested in your products or services.

Concept note-2: -A digital marketing platform is a solution that supports a variety of functions within the realm of marketing over the internet.

Concept note-3: -At its highest level, digital marketing has three main components: Lead Generation, Lead Capturing, and Lead Nurturing.

Concept note-4: -Digital marketing allows you to target a specific audience that will yield higher-quality leads that are more likely to become customers. Connecting your business with the most valuable leads will directly improve your conversion rate.
