What is Effective communication?

(A) ** Exchange of info so there is common understanding by all participants

(B) any form of communication used to inform

(C) any paid form of communication through mass media

(D) all of the above

(E) none are correct

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Effective communication definition is the process of exchanging or transmitting ideas, information, thoughts, knowledge, data, opinion, or messages from the sender through a selected method or channel to the receiver with a purpose that can be understood with clarity.

Concept note-2: -Using written copy, in the form of a blog, as well as video content, are two of the best types of communication channels for small-to-medium businesses. Content marketing allows you to do two important things for your business. First, it allows you to provide value to your customers.

Concept note-3: -Effective Communication is supplementary to marketing. It makes marketing campaign memorable and develops an emotional link between the marketer and target audience. When a piece of communication is to the point, relevant, worthwhile, and compelling, it moves audience-prospect-to consumer.

Concept note-4: -For communication to be effective, it must be clear, correct, complete, concise, and compassionate. We consider these to be the 5 C’s of communication, though they may vary depending on who you’re asking.
