Which of the following is a reason that a business would make changes to its products?

(A) ** To keep up with changing consumer preferences

(B) To make room for other products

(C) To spread risk over a wider area

(D) To predict the success of the changed product

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -by changing the selling price of products or bringing out budget products to match the state of the economy-eg during a recession, customers will spend less money and businesses may need to reduce their prices or develop a budget range to encourage customers to continue to purchase.

Concept note-2: -Businesses can respond to these changes by reviewing what they offer, and exploring whether they can or should make changes to catch up with consumer preferences. It may also be a good idea for some businesses to choose to promote the most affordable version of their products.
