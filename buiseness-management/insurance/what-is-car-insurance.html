What is car insurance?

(A) ** Motor insurance

(B) Life insurance

(C) Home insurance

(D) Travel insurance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Private Car Insurance Policy This is motor insurance that needs to be taken for any private car owned by an individual and is mandated by the Government of India. It covers the vehicle for damages against accidents, fire, natural disasters, theft among others and also covers for any injury to the owner.

Concept note-2: -motor vehicle insurance, also called automotive insurance, a contract by which the insurer assumes the risk of any loss the owner or operator of a car may incur through damage to property or persons as the result of an accident.

Concept note-3: -There are three different types of insurance-Private Car Insurance, Two-wheeler Insurance, and Commercial Vehicle Insurance.

Concept note-4: -Motor Insurance deals with the insurance covers for the loss or damage caused to the automobile or its parts due to natural and man-made calamities. It provides accident cover for individual owners of the vehicles while driving and also for passengers and third-party legal liability.
