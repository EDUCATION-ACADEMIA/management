Which of the following does NOT effect the cost of your car insurance?

(A) Your Age

(B) Gender

(C) Driving Record

(D) ** Drivers Test

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Some factors that may affect your auto insurance premiums are your car, your driving habits, demographic factors and the coverages, limits and deductibles you choose. These factors may include things such as your age, anti-theft features in your car and your driving record.

Concept note-2: -The price of your car insurance premium also increases with the add-ons that you purchase such as zero-depreciation cover, cost of vehicle towing in case of a breakdown, increased third party liability, protection of NCB benefit, and many others.

Concept note-3: -In case your car is vandalized or broken, your insurer will pay for the damages caused to your car, but it will not pay for any theft or damage caused to your personal belongings kept in the car, like your laptop, phone, or other valuables.
