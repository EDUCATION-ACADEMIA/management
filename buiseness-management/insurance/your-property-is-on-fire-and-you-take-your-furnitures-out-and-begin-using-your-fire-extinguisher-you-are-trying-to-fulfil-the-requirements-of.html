EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Remember to GET OUT, STAY OUT and CALL 9-1-1 or your local emergency phone number. Yell “Fire!” several times and go outside right away. If you live in a building with elevators, use the stairs. Leave all your things where they are and save yourself.

Concept note-2: -All fires can be extinguished by cooling, smothering, starving or by interrupting the combustion process to extinguish the fire.
