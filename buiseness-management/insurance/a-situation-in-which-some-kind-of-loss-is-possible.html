INSURANCE

[SOURCES]
A situation in which some kind of loss is possible

(A) ** Risk

(B) Peril

(C) Liability

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A RISK is a potential for a LOSS. The LOSS is the realization of that negative potential. A RISK is running across a busy street blindfolded. A LOSS is getting hit by a car while doing that.

Concept note-2: -Pure risk refers to risks that are beyond human control and result in a loss or no loss with no possibility of financial gain.

Concept note-3: -A speculative risk has the potential to result in a gain or a loss.

Concept note-4: -Risk can be categorized as to what causes the risk, and to whom it affects. Pure risk is a risk in which there is only a possibility of loss or no loss-there is no possibility of gain. Pure risk can be categorized as personal, property, or legal risk.
