INSURANCE

[SOURCES]
This type of insurance protects businesses if their property is, for example, stolen or damaged by fire.

(A) ** Property insurance

(B) Liability Insurance

(C) Health Insurance

(D) Business Owner’s Policy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commercial insurance helps the business owner pay the repair or replacement cost of any damaged property.

Concept note-2: -The property insurance policy is the insurance that protects the physical goods and the equipment of the business or home against any loss from theft, fire, and any other perils.

Concept note-3: -Different types of general insurance include motor insurance, health insurance, travel insurance, and home insurance.

Concept note-4: -Property insurance is a type of insurance that aids in defending the tangible assets of a person’s personal or commercial property from unforeseen events. The three types of property insurance coverage are replacement cost, actual cash value, and extended replacement costs.
