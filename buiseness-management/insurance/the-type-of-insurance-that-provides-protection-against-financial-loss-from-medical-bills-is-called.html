The type of insurance that provides protection against financial loss from medical bills is called

(A) ** health insurance

(B) coinsurance

(C) Premium

(D) life insurance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Health insurance is a form of insurance policy that covers the costs of medical treatment. Health insurance policies either cover or repay the cost of treatment for any included disease or injury. Various forms of health insurance cover a wide range of medical bills.

Concept note-2: -Mediclaim is a type of health insurance plan that takes care of the policy-holder’s healthcare expenses in case he/she gets hospitalized due to an illness or an accident. These policies cover the policyholder for a defined time period and up to a predetermined limit known as the ‘sum assured’.

Concept note-3: -Total Permanent Disability (TPD) Insurance Cover.

Concept note-4: -Health insurance is a type of insurance that covers medical expenses that arise due to an illness. These expenses could be related to hospitalisation costs, cost of medicines or doctor consultation fees.
