EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Purpose of insurance Its aim is to reduce financial uncertainty and make accidental loss manageable. It does this substituting payment of a small, known fee-an insurance premium-to a professional insurer in exchange for the assumption of the risk a large loss, and a promise to pay in the event of such a loss.

Concept note-2: -An insurance premium is the amount of money that an individual or business must pay for an insurance policy. The insurance premium is income for the insurance company, once it is earned, and also represents a liability in that the insurer must provide coverage for claims being made against the policy. Deductible.
