A contract between the insurance company and the insured that states the exact terms including what risks are covered is a(n)?

(A) premium

(B) ** policy

(C) agent

(D) deductible

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1) An insurance policy is a contract between the insurer and the insured. 2) The insured is the person whose life is being covered against the risk under the policy.

Concept note-2: -An insurance policy is a legal contract between the insurance company (the insurer) and the person(s), business, or entity being insured (the insured). Reading your policy helps you verify that the policy meets your needs and that you understand your and the insurance companyâ€™s responsibilities if a loss occurs.

Concept note-3: -A document containing terms and conditions of insurance contract is called policy.

Concept note-4: -The Principle of Indemnity The insurer (provider) compensates the insured (policyholder). The insurance company promises to compensate the policyholder for the amount of the loss up to the amount agreed upon in the contract.

Concept note-5: -Insurance policy document is proof of insurance. It is a formal legal document that is used as a legal binding for the insurance contract. The policy document is the evidence of the insurance contract and contains all the terms and conditions.
