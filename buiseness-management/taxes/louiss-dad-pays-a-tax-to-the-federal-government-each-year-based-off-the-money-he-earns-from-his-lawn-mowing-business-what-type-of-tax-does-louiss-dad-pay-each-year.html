TAXES

[SOURCES]
Louis’s dad pays a tax to the federal government each year based off the money he earns from his lawn mowing business. What type of tax does Louis’s dad pay each year?

(A) Payroll Tax

(B) ** Income Tax

(C) Sales Tax

(D) Property Tax

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Taxes are of two different types; direct tax and indirect tax. Taxes charged on income earned is called direct tax. Income tax is an excellent example of direct tax. On the other hand, an indirect tax is imposed by the government on a taxpayer for goods and services rendered.

Concept note-2: -Income tax is levied on the income earned by an individual or a family, whereas expenditure tax is levied on the expenditure incurred by an individual or a family. Also, expenditure tax is progressive in nature, whereas income tax is not.

Concept note-3: -An income tax is a tax imposed on individuals or entities (taxpayers) in respect of the income or profits earned by them (commonly called taxable income). Income tax generally is computed as the product of a tax rate times the taxable income.
