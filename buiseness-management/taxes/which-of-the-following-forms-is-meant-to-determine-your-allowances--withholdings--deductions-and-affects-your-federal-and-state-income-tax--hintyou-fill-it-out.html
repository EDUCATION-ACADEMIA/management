Which of the following forms is meant to determine your allowances / withholdings / deductions and affects your FEDERAL and STATE income tax %? Hint:YOU fill it out

(A) W2 Form

(B) ** W4 Form

(C) 1040EZ

(D) I9 Form

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The W-4 Form is an IRS form that you complete to let your employer know how much money to withhold from your paycheck for federal taxes.

Concept note-2: -The W-4 form tells the employer the amount of tax to withhold from an employee’s paycheck based on their marital status, number of allowances and dependents, and other factors. The W-4 is also called an Employee’s Withholding Allowance Certificate. 1

Concept note-3: -Complete Form W-4 so that your employer can withhold the correct federal income tax from your pay. Consider completing a new Form W-4 each year and when your personal or financial situation changes.
