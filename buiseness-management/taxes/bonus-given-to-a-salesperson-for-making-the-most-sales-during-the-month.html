Bonus given to a salesperson for making the most sales during the month

(A) ** Taxable

(B) Nontaxable

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans. Bonus is taxed under the “income from salary” head.

Concept note-2: -When a bonus is declared by the employer, it is considered taxable in the financial year in which it is declared even though you might receive it in the next financial year.

Concept note-3: -While bonuses are subject to income taxes, they don’t simply get added to your income and taxed at your top marginal tax rate. Instead, your bonus counts as supplemental income and is subject to federal withholding at a 22% flat rate.
