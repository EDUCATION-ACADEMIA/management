(A) payroll withholdings

(B) income tax

(C) social security

(D) ** year to date

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gross pay is what employees earn before taxes, benefits and other payroll deductions are withheld from their wages. The amount remaining after all withholdings are accounted for is net pay or take-home pay.

Concept note-2: -Payroll deductions are wages withheld from an employee’s paycheck for the payment of taxes, benefits, or garnishments. There are both mandatory and voluntary payroll deductions. The order in which deductions are taken out of paychecks also matters because some are made pre-tax and some are made post-tax.
