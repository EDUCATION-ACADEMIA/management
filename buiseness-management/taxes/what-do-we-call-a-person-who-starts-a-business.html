What do we call a person who starts a business?

(A) ** Entrepreneur

(B) Enterprise

(C) Entreposuer

(D) Extraposer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A person who undertakes the risk of starting a new business venture is called an entrepreneur. An entrepreneur creates a firm to realize their idea, known as entrepreneurship, which aggregates capital and labor in order to produce goods or services for profit.

Concept note-2: -Startups are founded by one or more entrepreneurs who want to develop a product or service for which they believe there is demand. These companies generally start with high costs and limited revenue, which is why they look for capital from a variety of sources such as venture capitalists.

Concept note-3: -An entrepreneur is a person who starts a new business. What is entrepreneurship? Entrepreneurship is the process of starting a new business.
