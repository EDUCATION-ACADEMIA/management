Which of the following supply involving movement of goods is an intra-state supply?

(A) Location of supplier in Kerala and place of supply in Tamilnadu

(B) ** Location of supplier in Karnataka and place of supply in Karnataka

(C) Location of supplier in Kerala and place of supply on Andhra Pradesh

(D) c) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An intrastate supply is a supply of goods or services inside the same state or Union territory that is subject to GST. Intrastate supply would not apply to goods or services provided to a Special Economic Zone developer or Special Economic Zone unit located within the same state.

Concept note-2: -The term “Inter-State Supply of Products” refers to a supply of goods where the supplier and the recipient are in different states or union territories. Intra-State delivery of products, on the other hand, refers to goods delivered to a location within the same State or Union area as the supplier.

Concept note-3: -For example, if you have a company ABC in the State of Karnataka and you supply products to Company XYZ in Madhya Pradesh, then it comes under the category of inter-state supply. It ensures that the trade would become an inter-state supply if the supplier ‘s position and the delivery place are in separate countries.

Concept note-4: -Whereas, intra-State supply of goods means supply of goods where the location of the supplier and place of supply are in the same State or Union territory.
