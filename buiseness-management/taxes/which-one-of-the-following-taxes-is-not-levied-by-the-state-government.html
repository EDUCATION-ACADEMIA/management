Which one of the following taxes is not levied by the State Government?

(A) Entertainment tax

(B) VAT

(C) Professional tax

(D) ** None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Profession Tax.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Excise Tax.

Concept note-3: -The taxation system in India empowers the state governments to levy income tax on agricultural income, professional tax, value added tax (VAT), state excise duty, land revenue and stamp duty.

Concept note-4: -Land revenue is not levied by the Union Government or Central government.
