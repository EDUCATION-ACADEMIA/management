The primary sources of income for young people between 18 and 30 who are not living on a pension (retirement) or social security are?

(A) Profits from businesses they own.

(B) Dividends and interest from investments

(C) Rents

(D) ** Wages, Salaries, and Tips

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Determine your retirement income sources Guaranteed Income (i.e. Social Security, Annuities) Pension plans (i.e., defined benefit plans) IRAs. Retirement savings, including 401(k), 403(b), and 457 plans.

Concept note-2: -▪ Primary Income. ➢earnings arising from the provision of a factor of production: labor, Financial assets, land, and natural resources.

Concept note-3: -The amount of pension is 50% of the emoluments or average emoluments whichever is beneficial. Minimum pension presently is Rs. 9000 per month. Maximum limit on pension is 50% of the highest pay in the Government of India (presently Rs. 1, 25, 000) per month. Pension is payable up to and including the date of death.
