TAXES

[SOURCES]
Tax money should be used by each level of government to ____

(A) make politicians rich

(B) pay for luxury items like private planes

(C) ** provide goods and services that are needed by the community

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Taxes are used by the government for carrying out various welfare schemes including employment programmes. There are Lakhs of employees in various departments and the administrative cost has to be borne by the Government.

Concept note-2: -Sales tax: A consumption tax imposed by a government on the sale of goods and services.

Concept note-3: -The Government of India earns tax and non-tax revenues to carry out various functions like providing public goods, implementing welfare schemes related to education and food, and managing the day-to-day functions of the country.

Concept note-4: -Answer: GST is one indirect tax for the whole nation, which will make India one unified common market. GST is a single tax on the supply of goods and services, right from the manufacturer to the consumer.
