Sarah earns a gross wage of €24, 100 in her first year of working. She pays 20% in income tax and is entitled to a tax credit of €3, 300. Calculate her net pay.

(A) €32480

(B) €29180

(C) €25880

(D) ** €22580

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -That means that your net pay will be ₹ 88, 000 per year, or ₹ 7, 333 per month. Your average tax rate is 12.0% and your marginal tax rate is 12.0%. This marginal tax rate means that your immediate additional income will be taxed at this rate.
