The total final taxes on passive income for the year is

(A) P13, 000

(B) 17, 000

(C) ** 11, 750

(D) 9, 875

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Unification of tax rates on passive income-a uniform tax rate of 15 percent will be imposed on interest income, dividends, and capital gains on the sale of shares of stock, debt instruments and other securities not traded in a local exchange or an organized marketplace.

Concept note-2: -According to the new tax regime, if your annual income ranges from Rs.10.00 lakhs to Rs.12.50 lakh, the tax deduction rate will be Rs.75, 000 + 20% above Rs.10, 00, 000.

Concept note-3: -Yes, the IRS does collect taxes on passive income.

Concept note-4: -Dividends received by a non-resident foreign corporation from a domestic corporation are subject to a general final WHT at the rate of 25%.
