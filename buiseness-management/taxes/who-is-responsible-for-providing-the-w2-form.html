Who is responsible for providing the W2 form?

(A) You

(B) ** Your Employer

(C) Your bank

(D) The IRS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -W-2 employer requirements necessitate that employers fill out the W-2 Form and hand over copies to employees, plus send copies to the SSA or Social Security Administration.

Concept note-2: -Employers must complete, file electronically or by mail with the Social Security Administration (SSA), and furnish to their employees Form W-2, Wage and Tax StatementPDF showing the wages paid and taxes withheld for the year for each employee.

Concept note-3: -Employers (W-2) withhold money from your pay and send it to the various tax agencies throughout the tax year on your behalf.
