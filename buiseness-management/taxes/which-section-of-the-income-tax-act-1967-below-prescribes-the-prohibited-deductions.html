Which Section of the Income Tax Act 1967 below prescribes the prohibited deductions?

(A) Section 33

(B) Section 34A

(C) ** Section 39

(D) Section 40

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Following Budget 2019, Section 39(1)(r) was introduced into the Income Tax Act 1967 (ITA) to provide that a tax deduction will not be allowed on payments by Malaysian residents to any Labuan company, subject to any rules that may be prescribed by the Minister of Finance (MoF).

Concept note-2: -Section 33 ITA and the relevant case law provides clear principles on what can or cannot be deducted from your gross income.

Concept note-3: -127. (1) Notwithstanding any other provision of this Act but subject to section 127A, any income specified in Part I of Schedule 6 shall, subject to this section, be exempt from tax.
