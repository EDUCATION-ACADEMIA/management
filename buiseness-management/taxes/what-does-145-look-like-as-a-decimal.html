What does 1.45% look like as a decimal?

(A) 1.45

(B) 0.145

(C) ** 0.0145

(D) 0.00145

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How do You Write a 1.45% as a Decimal? You write the percentage as 0.0145 in decimal notation. What is 1.45 Percent in Decimal Form? In decimal form, 1.45 percent equals 0.0145.

Concept note-2: -To convert 1.45 to percent multiply 1.45 by 100. The result is 145 percent, or, using the percent sign, 145 %.

Concept note-3: -You write the percentage as 0.026 in decimal notation.

Concept note-4: -In this problem, 2.5 percent can be expressed in decimal form as . 025.

Concept note-5: -Remember that the decimal point is located between the ones and the tenths place. 45% is written as 0.45.
