Which of the following documents is most useful when planning out a whole project?

(A) Diary

(B) To-do list

(C) Timeline

(D) ** Gantt chart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DUE DATES: The dates are one of the most essential aspects of a Gantt chart since they show project managers not only when the project will start and end, but also when each job will take place. These are shown at the top of the graph.

Concept note-2: -Gantt charts are useful for planning and scheduling projects. They help you assess how long a project should take, determine the resources needed, and plan the order in which you’ll complete tasks. They’re also helpful for managing the dependencies between tasks.
